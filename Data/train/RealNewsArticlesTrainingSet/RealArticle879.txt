Khloe Kardashian might have dropped a clue she was pregnant months ago Khloe Kardashian may have hinted she was expecting a child long before news of her pregnancy emerged.

The reality star — whose pregnancy was first reported Tuesday by Us Weekly — posted a Polaroid photo to Snapchat in late June that showed her with her boyfriend, Cleveland Cavaliers forward Tristan Thompson, with the words “Dad + Mom” written on the bottom.

Another Polaroid snap from that night was dated June 25, and Kardashian uploaded all of them to Instagram on June 26.

Khloe Kardashian shared this photo collage in June.

“I’ve never had a surprise party in my life! I was blown away!” Kardashian tweeted on June 26, referring to her 33rd birthday party from the night before. “Im so lucky and blessed! Thank you so much baby!!! You treat me like a queen!”

Kardashian is reportedly four months pregnant.

That said, there’s plenty of evidence that the Polaroid photo wasn’t meant to be taken literally. People close to Kardashian and Thompson told TMZ at the time that “Mom” and “Dad” were simply syrupy nicknames they used for each other.

Kardashian also suggested she was hungover in a tweet on the same day she posted the “Dad + Mom” picture.

The reality star revealed over the summer that she had gotten off of birth control during a testimonial segment on “Keeping up with the Kardashians.”

“He wants to have about five or six kids with me and that’s lovely,” Kardashian said on the show. “We could start at one and we could grow from there. But now knowing I’m not on birth control is scary. It’s like a really big step.”

Her pregnancy news came less than a week after reports emerged that her 20-year-old half-sister Kylie Jenner is pregnant as well. Her older sister Kim, meanwhile, is reportedly expecting a child via surrogate.
