Kendall Jenner Thinks It's 'a Bit Weird' That Kylie Had a Baby Before Her Kendall Jenner is now the only member of the Kardashian family who doesn't have a child. Kim and her husband Kanye West have three: North, Saint, and Chicago. Kourtney co-parents Mason, Penelope, and Reign with her ex partner Scott Disick. Rob Kardashian has a daughter, Dream, with his ex Blac Chyna. Kendall's younger sister, Kylie, welcomed her first baby (a daughter named Stormi Webster) with boyfriend Travis Scott in February. And just last month, Khloé Kardashian welcomed her first child (also a daughter, named True) with boyfriend Tristan Thompson.

In a new interview with Elle magazine, Kendall admits that even she can barely keep up with the amount of babies joining the Kardashian-Jenner group. "[My family is] forever growing,” Jenner said while chatting with Lana Del Rey for the magazine. “Every time I think it’s eased up, someone’s popping out a baby.”

Kendall specifically talked about Kylie's birth during her interview. “It’s obviously a bit weird that your little sister is having a baby before you,” she said. “I didn’t expect it to happen like this. But it’s beautiful.” Not only has Kylie taken to motherhood well, Kendall says it's also made her a more loving sister in general. “It’s brought us closer together. We’ve always been very close, but we would butt heads all the time. This has made her a bit more loving toward me," she said.
