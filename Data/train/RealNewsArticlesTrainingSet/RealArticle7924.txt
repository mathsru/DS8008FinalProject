Here's How Nicki Minaj Allegedly Reacted To Cardi B And Meek Mill's Song 2018 was a rough year for Philadelphia's reformed phenom Meek Mill. However, everyone loves a great comeback story. Early Friday morning (Nov. 30), he blessed the hip-hop world with his fourth studio album, Championships.

After being released from prison this past spring, the "Wins & Losses" emcee became a prominent advocate for criminal reform and kept fans on the edge of their seats, beaming with anticipation, over the rumored features and the album's content. The album features budding R&B songstress, Ella Mai, Future, foe-turned-friend, Drake, and even the GOAT himself, Jay-Z, who had a word or two about Kanye West.

It also features a banger, "On Me," with Bronx femcee Cardi B, noted rival of his ex-girlfriend, Nicki Minaj. Back in September, it was reported that the pair's professional relationship grew and they were working together, so it's evident the rumors were true.
