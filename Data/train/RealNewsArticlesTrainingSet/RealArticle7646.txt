It's an aca-engagement! "Pitch Perfect" costars Anna Camp and Skylar Astin are getting married.

Astin, who plays loveable Treblemaker Jesse in the a-cappella-singing films, appeared to pop the question while the couple was vacationing in Kauai, Hawaii, over the holiday weekend.

"I asked. She said yes!!!" the 28-year-old wrote on Instagram on Saturday, captioning a pic of his fiancee showing off her new sparkler.

The "True Blood" actress, 33, reposted the same pic that she captioned, "He asked. I said YES!!!!"

On Sunday, Camp took to Twitter to thank her fans for their well wishes.

"Thanks for all the celebration and love!" she wrote. "My heart is super warm today"

The pair has been dating since June 2013 after co-starring in the 2012 breakout comedy. Though they didn't play each other's love interests -- Astin was paired with Oscar nominee Anna Kendrick in the film -- the pair have given fans couples envy on their social media feeds with sweet snaps of their relationship. Camp also made a cameo as Aubrey, a tightly wound Barden Bella, in this summer's sequel, though it's unclear if either will appear in "Pitch Perfect 3," which is set for 2017.
