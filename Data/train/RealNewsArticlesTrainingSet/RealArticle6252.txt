List of awards and nominations received by NSYNC This is a list of awards received by American pop group *NSYNC. The group has won three American Music Awards, five Billboard Music Awards, seven MTV Video Music Awards and has received nine Grammy Award nominations. Additionally, they have received two RIAA Diamond certifications. *NSYNC received a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame in April 2018.[1]
