Kardashian sisters visit Planned Parenthood, voice their support for organization It remains to be seen whether or not she’s gearing up for Kanye West’s mythical 2020 presidential run, but Kim Kardashian isn’t shying away from politics.

The “Keeping Up with the Kardashians” star, with sisters Khloe and Kourtney in tow, stopped by Planned Parenthood Los Angeles Friday to learn about the organization and relay their newfound information to fans.

“My sisters and I visited Planned Parenthood recently and learned that the House of Representatives forced through a bill that strips health care coverage from millions of people and raises health care costs, including Planned Parenthood patients,” she wrote on Instagram, accompanying her photo with a sad emoji. “They are such an amazing place that provides so much to so many. #istandwithpp”

Planned Parenthood faces an uncertain future, as the health care act currently being pushed by the Trump administration would, if passed, eliminate the organization’s funding for screenings and treatments. Last week, the bill passed in the House of Representatives, but it still needs to go through the Senate.

Khloe shared a photo of the visit on Instagram, captioning it, “Visited @PPLosAngeles yesterday and learned so much. 2.5MM rely on PP for care -birth control, cancer-screenings, STD testing and so much more! #IStandWithPP.”

This isn’t the first time the 36-year-old mom of two has gotten political. She supported Hillary Clinton in the 2016 election, and has previously used Twitter as a platform to express her views on gun violence, urging her fans in June 2016 to demand gun control from Congress.
