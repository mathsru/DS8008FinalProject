Best Sock Boots for Fall and Winter Every fall and winter, an endearing shoe style pops up and prevails above the rest—whether it’s a statement mule in early autumn, a satin ballet flat toward the end of summer, or a comfortable loafer that transcends every season and weather transition. This season, however, we’re looking toward a reworked classic and nominating the best sock boots as the essential winter shoe. Take ’s offerings, for example: Season after season, the label’s Strada and Aria sock boots are some of street style’s favorites for a modern and unique look, regardless of the runway’s dominant shoe trends.

At this point, with the range of structured leather boots on the market, a sock boot seems almost irregular. Still, Nomasei co-founder Marine Braquet thinks the sock boot will endure. “The trend keeps coming back because it flatters the silhouette, so women never get fed up,” she explains. “Our clients often say, ‘It’s sexy but easy to wear!’ and it is—chic, and extremely comfortable when well made. All of our sock boots are timeless, so it’s a safe bet.”

Safe bet it is—modern twists on the sock boot work with virtually everything, from knitted maxi skirts and baggy denim to oversize suiting and jersey midi dresses (and they’re one of the most comfortable boot styles around). The best sock boots for women are as walkable as they are sleek. With styles ranging from extra-sharp pointed toes to knee boots that stretch comfortably over the leg, shop our favorite versions of the season ahead.
