All the Best Looks from the 2018 Film Independent Spirit Awards It’s that time of the year again. The 2025 awards season is in full swing, with the Golden Globes, the SAG Awards and the BAFTAs behind us, and the Academy Awards rapidly approaching on March 3. Last night, we had our eye on the 2025 Independent Spirit Awards red carpet. The award show, as its name suggests, is recognizes independent films and filmmakers. It is an homage to grit and creativity.

This show isn’t as formal as something like the Oscars, but that’s what makes it so interesting. The slightly more flexible dress code always inspires a variety of unique interpretations. This is the red carpet to come to completely as you see fit.

From track jacket skirt-sets to provocative little black dresses, this year’s red carpet was a runway for celebrity style exploration.
