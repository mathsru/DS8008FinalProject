Cameron Dallas Has An Intense Beauty Routine Beauty routines are not just for girls — guys like to look good too.

Model and influencer Cameron Dallas broke down his “simple” beauty routine in a new vid, after posing for Mario Testino‘s Towel Series.

“For me, I like to feel great. I like to look great. When it comes to my beauty regime, I like to keep it just simple,” he shared — but “simple” in this case is actually not simple at all.

In the vid, Cameron asks about his jade roller — which he uses across his face is an ancient Chinese skin-care tool that helps alleviate under-eye circles and depuff the face.

Then, there’s ice-water face dunks: “Getting ready shouldn’t really take all morning, just kind of a splash of water on your face,” he says, before, you know, dunking his face into freezing cold ice water more than once.

Cameron is also a big advocate of using natural ingredients in his products: “If you can’t eat it, don’t put it on your face. I’m really big on using products that don’t have anything harmful to the environment—you know, methane and just fumes from gas and things like that.”
