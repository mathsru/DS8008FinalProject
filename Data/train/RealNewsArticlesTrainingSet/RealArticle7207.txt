A guide to Prince Harry's ex-girlfriends The pair were reportedly introduced in 2009 through a mutual friend. At the time, Harry was 25 years old and Flack was 31 and cohosted a popular British TV show, "Gladiators."

Flack wrote in her autobiography, "Storm in a C Cup," that "once the story got out, that was it. We had to stop seeing each other. I was no longer Caroline Flack, TV presenter, I was Caroline Flack, Prince Harry's bit of rough."

Flack died on February 15, 2020, at the age of 40. In August, a coroner's inquest into her death recorded it as a suicide.
