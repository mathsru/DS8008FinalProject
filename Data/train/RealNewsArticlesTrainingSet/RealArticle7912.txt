Can Dorit Kemsley and Lisa Vanderpump Move Past Those Comments About PK and Their Friendship? It seems like something was lost in translation when Lisa Vanderpump told Dorit Kemsley much of their friendship is based on the fact that the West Hollywood restaurateur is close pals with Paul Kemsley, AKA PK. Earlier this season on The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills, LVP told Dorit: "I love you — mainly on the strength of loving your husband and wanting to love you and make it right." And now we're learning a bit more about that quip from Dorit: "I think that that was twisted a little," she shared on Watch What Happens Live with Andy Cohen on Tuesday.

Dorit previously addressed the moment in an interview with E!
