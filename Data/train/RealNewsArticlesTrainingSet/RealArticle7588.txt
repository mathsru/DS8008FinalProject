Venice Film Festival 2017: The best red carpet looks Stars dressed to impress at the 74th Annual Venice Film Festival!

Hollywood's biggest names made the red carpet rounds at the annual event in Venice, Italy, and of course the fashion didn't disappoint.

Amal Clooney made her first red carpet appearance after giving birth to her twins back in June, wearing a dreamy lavender gown from Atelier Versace. She paired the look with Aquazzura shoes.

Jane Fonda was a total showstopper in a dusty blue tulle Marchesa gown with floral embroidery, while Amanda Seyfried stunned in a floral Alexander McQueen frock.

Matt Damon also made several red carpet appearances with his gorgeous wife Luciana alongside him. Of course, Luciana stole the spotlight from her husband of 12 years not once, but twice! She donned a Atelier Versace gown in a bright red hue for her first red carpet. Days later, she dazzled in a emerald green Marchesa gown.

The annual film festival is going on through September 9, so there are definitely more red carpet looks to come! Check out the slideshow for the best Venice Film Festival ensembles so far.
