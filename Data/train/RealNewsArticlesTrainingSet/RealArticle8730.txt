Prince Harry news and updates on Meghan and the Royal baby Prince Harry

Prince Harry, Duke of Sussex, is a member of the British royal family and the younger son of Prince Charles and the late Princess Diana. He served in the British Army and is known for his charitable work, including founding the Invictus Games for wounded veterans. Harry married Meghan Markle in 2018, and they have two children, Archie and Lilibet. The couple stepped back from royal duties in 2020 and now reside in California.
