All the looks from the 2017 Victoria's Secret Fashion Show The annual Victoria's Secret Fashion Show took place on November 20 in Shanghai, China, and aired on CBS Tuesday.

Although preparations for the show didn't exactly go smoothly — with Gigi Hadid dropping out and reported visa issues making it impossible for models and performers like Katy Perry to attend — the event indeed went on, and all the models slayed on the catwalk. Even model Ming Xi, who recovered gracefully after taking a fall.

If you missed the glamorous event, don't worry; we've compiled a roundup of the fashion show featuring newcomers like Estelle Chen and Angels like Adriana Lima, and all the stunning outfits they wore to walk down the runway.
