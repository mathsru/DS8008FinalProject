Kaia Gerber and Gabriel-Kane Day Lewis Star in Hudson Denim Ad Los Angeles-based denim brand Hudson used two icon descendants, Kaia Gerber, the daughter of supermodel Cindy Crawford, and Gabriel-Kane Day Lewis, the son of actress Daniel Day-Lewis, as the new faces of its Fall 2017 campaign.

Photographed by renowned photographer Patrick Demarchelier, with art direction by David Lipman, this ad makes Kaia and Gabriel-Kane's first-ever apparel brand campaign together. What a win for Hudson. Founded in 2002 by Peter Kim, the premium denim and apparel brand is celebrating its 15-year anniversary — and what better way to do so than featuring the next generation of fresh faces?

What's more? Kaia Gerber is the spitting image of her mother Cindy Crawford in the photos, and one of the poses is reminiscent of Cindy's iconic photo shoots. The Hudson Fall 2017 campaign launches this August across multiple media platforms including billboards and social media, and it will be displayed across multiple media outlets digitally throughout the season.
