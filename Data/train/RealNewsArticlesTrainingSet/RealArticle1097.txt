MTV VMAs 2017: Inside all of the after parties After the party comes the after party!

The MTV VMAs is arguably the music world's biggest party of the year, so the after parties definitely don't disappoint. After Sunday night's 2017 Video Music Awards, music's hottest stars descended on a slew of star-studded after parties, and most of them made quick outfit changes that they felt more comfortable letting loose in.

Hailey Baldwin slipped into an oversized vintage t-shirt, while Hailee Steinfeld rocked a spaghetti strap white dress with a pink accent and over-the-knee boots. She posed with "Stranger Things" actress Millie Bobby Brown, who rocked a metallic top tucked into high-waisted black slacks.

Social media star Chantel Jeffries came this close to suffering a wardrobe malfunction at the Republic Records/Cadillac party, where she rocked a black ensemble that featured a thigh-high slit and a cleavage-baring neckline that plunged past her bellybutton.
