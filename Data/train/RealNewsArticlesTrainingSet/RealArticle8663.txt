Katy Perry and Orlando Bloom Take Their Rekindled Romance to Prague When reconnecting with an ex (again), it’s best to do so in a foreign country where there are no friends or loved ones to openly side-eye your decision. Unfortunately for Katy Perry and Orlando Bloom, they are very famous, which is why the whole world has been keeping up with their Czech reunion in real time.

The couple, who broke up last February after dating for a year, are currently on vacation in Prague, where Bloom is shooting the Amazon series Carnival Row. They’ve been photographed walking Bloom’s dog, Mighty, as well as touring the city’s castle. And for those wondering, it’s too cold in Prague for any paddle boarding.

The trip is actually their second together this year. Bloom and Perry quietly met in the Maldives after Perry had wrapped her performance in Dubai, where fans photographed the couple attempting to lay low, Leonardo DiCaprio-style, in baseball caps.

There was also the Ed Sheeran concert last August, where fans caught the pair once again. They left the show together on Bloom’s motorcycle.

Perry addressed her relationship with Bloom on SiriusXM’s Morning Mash Up that same month, saying, “It’s nice to keep people you love around you. When you get older, lines get blurred.”
