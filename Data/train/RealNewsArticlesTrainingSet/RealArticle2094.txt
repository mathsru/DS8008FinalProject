Nick Jonas & Mustard Bring Otherworldly 'Anywhere' to 2018 MTV Movie & TV Awards Nick Jonas and Mustard transformed the MTV Movie and TV Awards into an otherworldly laser lights extravaganza they brought their collab “Anywhere” to the stage at the Tiffany Haddish-hosted show.

The performance opened with Jonas singing into the camera while neon wisps of smoke drifted behind him. The camera then pulled back to reveal Mustard standing on a raised platform while pink, purple and blue laser lights exploded around him. The backdrop behind them flashed interstellar CGI imagery of alien desert landscapes and striking geometric figures while Jonas crooned the moody dance track and Mustard hyped the crowd.

Jonas was also nominated for a Golden Popcorn trophy at the Monday (June 18) night show as an actor; he was up for best on-screen team as part of the cast of Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle. The JoBro lost the award to the Losers Club from It, but at least his performance was a victory.
