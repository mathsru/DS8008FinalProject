Kim Kardashian Just Dyed Her Hair Bright Pink Kim Kardashian West, whose 17-hour transformation from brunette to platinum blond made headlines pretty much everywhere, has done it again. On Sunday she took to social media to announce her newest shade: a bright, bright, bright pink.

We had a hint something was coming on Saturday, when she basically sounded an alarm for a future color drop: "I can't even tell you how over my blond hair I am!" she tweet-raged. (As someone who is a very much dyed platinum blond too, I can attest that we all have those days.)

Kardashian West, however, did something about it. A big something. Although she hinted that she was a "brunette at heart" during a Create + Cultivate panel on Saturday, she definitely didn't go back to her roots with her recent dye job. Instead, she went bold—and it's pretty great.

Judging by the multiple reveal posts, she's excited about it too.
