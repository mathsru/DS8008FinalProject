Bruno Mars Gives Longtime Girlfriend a Shoutout at Grammys Bruno Mars won big at the 2018 Grammys on Sunday, January 28. While accepting the award for Record of the Year, Mars paid tribute to his stunning girlfriend, Jessica Caban.

"My lady, Jessica, I love you baby,” Mars gushed onstage. “Thank you for being my rock and being on my side throughout this whole process."
