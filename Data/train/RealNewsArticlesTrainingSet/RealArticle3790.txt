Niall Horan to Perform With Maren Morris at CMA Awards on Nov. 8 Niall Horan‘s debut solo album Flicker (out Oct. 20) is likely on its way to debuting at No. 1 on next week’s Billboard 200, and on Tuesday (Oct. 24), it was announced that he will bring his new music to life on stage at the 2017 CMA Awards on Nov. 8.

Country singer Maren Morris will be joining the Irish singer-songwriter, which lends itself to speculation that the song performed will be “Seeing Blind,” which features Morris on the album. (Morris is also accompanying Horan on his 2018 Flicker World Tour.)

“Can’t wait to share the stage with ya darling,” Horan tweeted in response to Morris announcing the performance. “Going to be a great night.”

Along with Flicker the album, Horan premiered a documentary, On the Record: Niall Horan – Flicker, exclusive to Apple Music.

Other performers for the 51st CMA Awards (to air on ABC) include Brad Paisley, Kane Brown, Kelsea Ballerini, Reba McEntire, Dierks Bentley and Rascal Flatts.
