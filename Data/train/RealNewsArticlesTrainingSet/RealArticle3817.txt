The Most Epic Sets of Celeb Brows Ever Anyone who's laid eyes on a runway, fashion campaign, movie screen, or beauty counter in the past five or so years knows it: We're living in the Arch Ages. That's right, we've fully embraced bigger, bolder, thicker, more emphatic eyebrows.

Thankfully, and perhaps refreshingly, fashion's obsession with "thin" hasn't applied to brows in quite some time. It goes without saying that this trend is largely epitomized by Cara Delevingne, who was attributed with a decrease in tweezer sales last year.

And let's not forget Teen Vogue cover girl Lily Collins, whose bewitching, bushy brows (shown here) have earned her comparisons to one of the trend's true pioneers: Audrey Hepburn.
