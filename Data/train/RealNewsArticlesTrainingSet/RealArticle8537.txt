Watch Chris Pratt Pet a Baby Raptor in the New “Jurassic World 2” Teaser Chris Pratt has been keeping a fairly low profile since splitting from Anna Faris back in August, but the actor is slowly but surely making his return to the limelight… in the cutest way possible. The 38-year-old is one of two stars featured in the upcoming Jurassic Park sequel, Fallen Kingdom, the other being a baby raptor!

Tweeted by the film’s writer and executive producer Colin Trevorrow, the six-second clip shows Pratt’s character Owen Grady encountering the tiny dinosaur.

“From our Jurassic family to yours,” Trevorrow said in his tweet, tagging Pratt along with costar Bryce Dallas Howard, as well as the film’s director, J. A. Bayona, and producer, Frank Marshall.

The dino seems friendly enough in the shot, nuzzling Grady’s hand and getting some chin scratches as though it were a family pet. It offers little to no insight about the plot of the film, but TBH, we wouldn’t mind if this were the entire movie. Just look at that little face!

it will definitely tide us over until the film’s actual trailer, which is rumored for release sometime after December 1, and its theater release date, which is set for June 22, 2018.
