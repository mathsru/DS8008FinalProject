Kim Kardashian reveals Chicago’s middle name - and the sweet reason behind it Kim Kardashian welcomed her third child, Chicago, on January 15 2018 via surrogate.

It seems like just yesterday that little Chi came into the world. Announcing the news on her website at the time, Kim exclaimed: "SHE'S HERE!"

"Kanye and I are happy to announce the arrival of our healthy, beautiful baby girl," she wrote.

"We are incredibly grateful to our surrogate who made our dreams come true with the greatest gift one could give and to our wonderful doctors and nurses for their special care."

"North and Saint are especially thrilled to welcome their baby sister."

A little more about the woman who carried Kim's child was revealed after she was introduced to the family on an episode of Keeping Up With The Kardashians.

On the season 14 finale, La'Reina Haynes made her on-screen debut two months after giving birth to Kim and Kanye's third child. While her face wasn't shown on screen, she did speak to the family about how special it was - and she's giving us all the feels. Kris thanked La'Reina for the gesture, to which she responded:

"Thank you. I'm so proud of doing it in general.

"Like, with all the women who do have pregnancy issues, that put so much trust in people like me. It's amazing. It's already a big deal."

When asked if she found it difficult to carry another couple's baby, she explained,

"I like being pregnant. I know it sounds really weird,

"The pregnancy itself, you know, it just feels natural, like I'm supposed to do it. Because I know from the very beginning this is not my baby. And at the end, my joy and my satisfaction is with whoever I'm having the baby for,

"I know what my job is in all of it. It makes me feel important because I'm like, 'Wow, I did something so good.' This is beautiful. I would do this again a thousand more times."
