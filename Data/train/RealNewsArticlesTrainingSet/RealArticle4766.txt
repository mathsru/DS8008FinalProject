Kendall Jenner Is the Best Un-Dressed Celebrity at the Met Gala Kendall Jenner is no stranger to showing a little skin, whether in her street style or strutting down the Victoria’s Secret runway. When the model turned out on last night's Met Gala red carpet, she wore a glitter-flecked La Perla slip that seemed to be channeling Rihanna’s infamous naked dress from the 2014 CFDA/Vogue Fashion Fund awards. Jenner, who also stars in the brand’s latest lingerie campaign, opted to leave little to the imagination—especially with the dress’s open back, which revealed a major flash of a matching black thong to make a statement both coming and going.

Jenner’s slip also featured a bold cut-out across the front, which showed off her toned midriff and felt true to the night’s avant-garde theme. She extended the spangly statement to pointed pumps in the same finish. The choice of footwear felt like a more directional alternative to, say, last season’s transparent shoe, a Jenner style signature. Too many accessories would have been overkill, and Jenner did well to eschew them in favor of barely there makeup and not much else besides a knowing smile. Consider it a lesson in how to do boudoir-inspired dress on the red carpet and beyond.
