Tyler Henry Predicted Khloe Kardashian's Troubles with Tristan Thompson Months Ago What Is In Store for the Bravolebs? Tyler Henry Knows All...

The Hollywood Medium said he "saw some relationship issues" for the new parents of True Thompson.

Khloe Kardashian is the proud mama of a baby girl, True Thompson. But, as the saying goes: there's a thorn for every rose. And even as she basks in the delights of motherhood, the Keeping Up with the Kardashians sister is facing some romantic woes ... and Hollywood Medium's Tyler Henry totally saw it coming.

In an episode of the E! show that was filmed months ago, Kardashian sat down with her go-to psychic, who kept mentioning her next beau. "You keep saying my 'next boyfriend,'" Khloe noted. "Do you not see anything about my current boyfriend?"

At the time, Kardashian was dating Tristan Thompson, who — in case you missed it — is now rumored to have cheated on her with multiple women while she was pregnant. Awkward.

Henry predicted that travel might come between the then-new couple; and indeed, rumors indicate that Thompson may have cheated on Kardashian while on the road in New York City, Washington D.C., and Boston.

Earlier this week, Henry appeared on the Zach Sang Show. On his reading with Kardashian, he tactfully said: "Well, I saw some relationship issues."
