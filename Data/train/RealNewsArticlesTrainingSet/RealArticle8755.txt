Vicki Gunvalson Uses Bible Verses To Apologize To Eddie Judge On Tonight’s The Real Housewives of Orange County The Real Housewives of Orange County is only one episode in, but we’re happy to say that we have high hopes for this new season!

Tonight we have some classic Vicki Gunvalson, apologizing for her behavior while avoiding talking about the actual behavior/actions. Tamra Judge somehow convinces Eddie to sit down and listen to Vicki’s explanation and to at least consider accepting her apology. Vicki attempts this latest round of “I’m sorry” by referencing Bible verses that relate to their situation. This ought to be good.

While Vicki attempts to mend her relationship with Eddie and Tamra, she lands on Kelly Dodd‘s bad side. “Kelly is stunned to learn that Vicki and Steve set up her ex-husband Michael and confronts Vicki over the betrayal.”

Since Eddie isn’t allowed to do any heavy workouts due to his heart issues, Tamra takes over his fitness class. She introduces us and the other cast members to her new friend Gina Kirschenheiter, who recently moved to the OC from Long Island.

On tonight’s episode we’ll also see newbie Emily Simpson and her husband Shane celebrating their ninth wedding anniversary.

Shannon Beador heads to Mexico with her daughters for their first solo vacation since her divorce from David.
