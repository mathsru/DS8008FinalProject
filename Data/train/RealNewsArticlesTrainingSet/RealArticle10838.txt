'It's a Boy': Emilie de Ravin to Have a Baby Boy in Fall Emilie de Ravin is going to be a mom of two.

The 36-year-old Australian actress said in an Instagram post Friday that she's expecting a baby boy with fiancé Eric Bilitch in the fall.

De Ravin is already parent to 2-year-old daughter Vera Audrey with Bilitch. She announced her pregnancy by posting a photo of herself holding up a positive pregnancy test.

"Coming this fall...Baby boy makes 4! Eric & I could not be more excited to share our happy news with y'all & Vera is over the moon about becoming a big sister!" @ericbilitch," the star captioned the picture.

Bilitch, a writer and director, shared the same snapshot on his own account.

"Baby Boy de Ravin-Bilitch coming soon!!" he wrote.
