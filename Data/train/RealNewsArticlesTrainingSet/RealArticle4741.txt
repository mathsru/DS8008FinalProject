The Most Fascinating Fascinators (and Hats!) From Pippa Middleton's Wedding Fascinators and hats are a staple of summer wedding attire for British women—and whether they're specifically chosen to match an outfit (à la the Duchess of Cambridge, naturally, with her custom blush-pink fascinator) or picked to clash brilliantly, it seems like the more fanciful the form, the better. For the wedding of Duchess Catherine's sister, Pippa Middleton, on Saturday, guests turned out in an array of colorful options, from the chicest black to the most eye-popping oranges.
