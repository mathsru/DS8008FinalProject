10 Best Dressed: Week of November 5, 2018 Femininity and iconicity: herewith the twin aspirations of the world’s most stylish as they slunk into November. Cami Morrone channeled Maria Callas in Reem Acra’s voluminous rose jersey column, while Kiki Layne (in Prada), Mackenzie Foy (in Rodarte), and Keira Knightley (in Chanel Haute Couture) went full-on Cinderella in confections of chiffon, taffeta, and tulle. In Los Angeles for the LACMA gala Kim Kardashian West was throwback-supermodel sultry in vintage Gucci by Tom Ford and Lou Doillon was a dazzling update on Rita Hayworth in Alessandro Michele’s beaded golden stunner. Smart spins on black after dark? Look no further than Zoë Kravitz in an off-the-shoulder, pearl-adorned column by Gabriela Hearst, or Cate Blanchett in an asymmetric beauty by Clare Waight Keller for Givenchy. Another accolade for Givenchy: Meghan Markle , the Duchess of Sussex, who closed her Australian tour in the label’s pleated knit skirt and tailored sweater. Pregnant and on the move, she makes world-shaking style look so easy.
