Goodbye Princess locks: Kate Middleton has a new haircut and it's totally chic Kate Middleton definitely falls in the camp of famous people with a signature hairstyle. Over the years - bar a few extra highlights and a grown out fringe - she's stuck to her guns with her long, bouncy curls. So the new look she debuted at Wimbledon classifies as a pretty major makeover.

Getty Images

The Duchess of Cambridge arrived to kick off the tennis tournament today with her dark locks cut into a sleek, long bob.

By our expert eye we're going to guess it was the work of her long-term hairdresser Richard Ward. The evidence? It's been styled in her trademark bouncy waves, or as he'd call it: a Chelsea blowout.

Kudos to Kate for mixing things up.
