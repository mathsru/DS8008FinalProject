The Pearsons are hitting the streets.

In a new video from Billy on the Street, enthusiastic host Billy Eichner is joined by cast members from NBC's hit This Is Us. Viewers are treated to two members of the Big Three — Chrissy Metz and Justin Hartley — and their TV parents, Mandy Moore and Milo Ventimiglia. We're just going to assume Randall, aka Sterling K. Brown, is off with Beth and the kids on a magical summer vacay.

During their ambush, they ran into some avid fans and some less-than-zealous folks. One admirer of Moore's had the gall to ask if the actress/singer was single — in front of Jack no less. Unfortunately for the patron, their love story won't be a tale as sweet as Candy because Moore is taken.

"This Is Us, and this is sexual harassment if you ask me," Eichner cracked. Enjoy seeing these actors without the pressure of having to locate the nearest tissue box in the clip below.
