On Monday, model Bella Hadid walked for the second time at the Victoria's Secret Fashion Show in Shanghai!

At the show, Hadid suffered a nip slip while wearing a pale blue bustier during the Goddesses segment of the show.

Hadid didn't let the wardrobe mishap bother her, though — she was seen smiling throughout the show. Last year, she told People magazine, “I am not a very good smiler on cue. I have to practice that a lot.”

She joked on Instagram with a pic of her smiling on the runway, writing, “..this. just. happened. (...SHE SMILES!!!!😝)”

While rehearsing the show, the 21-year-old expressed her excitement, writing on Instagram, “Feeling so grateful...The energy of the girls, the team, backstage / front stage, the incredible fans were so beautifully intense and motivating. I can't wait for tomorrow.”

Last year, Bella revealed how she prepped for her debut, telling the publication, “I have to close my best friend and roommate's door and put a big mirror in front of it so she can't get out of her room so that I have a part of the apartment to walk and practice.
