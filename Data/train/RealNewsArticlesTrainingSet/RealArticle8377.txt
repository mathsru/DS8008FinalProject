Naya Rivera Is All Smiles at Her First Public Event Since Domestic Battery Arrest Naya Rivera is putting the past behind her.

The former Glee star was all smiles at her first public event since her domestic battery arrest last November, stepping out at YouTube's 2018 winter Television Critics Association press tour on Saturday.

Rivera, who was at the event to promote YouTube's Step Up revival series, High Water, rocked an embellished tank top, short, silky navy blue shirt and knee-high boots.

Frederick M. Brown/Getty Images

"TCA today for @stepupseries! Im all about this look," she later posted on Instagram.

Rivera re-filed for divorce from Ryan Dorsey in December after filing to dismiss the order in October. Things got heated between Rivera -- who originally filed for divorce in November 2016 -- and Dorsey in November before she was arrested in Kanawha County, West Virginia, for domestic battery against the actor.

Dorsey later spoke out about Rivera's arrest, asking that their family's privacy be respected during this "difficult time." Court documents obtained by ET in December show that the pair reached a custody agreement over their 2-year-old son, Josey, agreeing to share 50/50 joint custody. See more in the video below.
