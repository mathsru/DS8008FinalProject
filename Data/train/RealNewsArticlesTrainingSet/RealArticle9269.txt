The second biggest money earner the stars of Love It or List It, interior designer Hilary Farr and real estate agent David Visentin, who have a collective net worth of $7 million. They haven’t written books or launched any spin-off series, but still manage to rake in some big bucks.



As for Chip and Joanna Gaines, don’t worry that they won’t be able to put their kids through school.
