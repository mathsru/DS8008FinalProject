Zedd & Liam Payne Announce New Single 'Get Low' Trending on Billboard

Zedd and Liam Payne collaborated on a new track, titled “Get Low.” The song, which the pair announced on Instagram, will be available Thursday (July 6).

On Tuesday, Payne also posted a Spotify “pre-save” link ahead of the song’s release.

Explore Liam Payne Zedd See latest videos, charts and news

No teasers of “Get Low” have arrived yet, but Zedd recently revealed to Beats 1 that “it’s the definition of a summer song to me, personally” and added that it has an urban, “Drake-ish influenced sound.” Zedd describes it as a balance between Payne’s solo work and where he’s headed with his own music

“Get Low” follows Payne’s debut solo single, “Strip That Down” — a song the One Direction member has described as “an edgy, different song for me to do.” He made his first U.S. solo television appearance performing the tune on Jimmy Fallon’s Tonight Show in June.
