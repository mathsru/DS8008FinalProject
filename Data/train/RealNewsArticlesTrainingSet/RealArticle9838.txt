Weird things everyone ignores about Gwen & Blake's relationship Gwen Stefani and Blake Shelton are always together: at work on The Voice, in the recording studio for duets like "Go Ahead and Break My Heart," and just about everywhere else (especially Instagram and red carpets). The couple seem awfully in love, but there are a few strange facets to their hyper-public relationship, including the fact that Stefani had no idea who Shelton was for a long time.

"I didn't even know he existed before the show," Stefani told Andy Cohen on his SiriusXM Radio Andy show in September 2019. "I didn't even know that he was a human being on this planet." The ska-turned-pop icon and the proud hillbilly have since made it no secret that they're outright obsessed with one another, with Stefani even admitting to Hoda Kotb on Today that she went back and watched old interviews with Shelton after they'd started dating.

From duets to daddy duty to wearing one another on their feet (seriously), here are just a few of the weirdest things about this pairing.
