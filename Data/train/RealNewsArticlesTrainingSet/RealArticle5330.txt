Halle Berry and Alex Da Kid's relationship goes Instagram official Updated Sept. 20, 2017, 12:05 p.m. ET

Halle Berry stunned fans with photos of her leg (and butt) revealing dress at the Kingsman premiere in London Monday, and she's grabbing attention again with a cuddly, black-and-white shot with her new beau.

Berry made her relationship with British music producer Alex Da Kid Instagram official Tuesday with the romantic pic.

In the photo's caption, Berry simply wrote, "My balance."

Alex Da Kid also posted the photo with an almost identical caption Tuesday.
