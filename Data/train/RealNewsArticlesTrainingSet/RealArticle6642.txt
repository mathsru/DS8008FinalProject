Jenni Rivera Was Honored With a Boutique Grand Opening It’s hard to believe that it’s been almost five years since the talented Jenni Rivera was tragically killed in a plane crash in Mexico. The void of her absence is felt by many, especially now as yesterday would have been Rivera’s 48th birthday.

To commemorate the special occasion, Rivera’s family celebrated at the grand opening of the Jenni Rivera Boutique in Huntington Park, California on Sunday, July 2. The store is focused on “executing Jenni’s dream by providing fun, sporty, high-quality clothing and accessories for the voluptuous, bold, beautiful woman.”
