Zendaya slays in sexy new Bruno Mars video, 'Versace On The Floor' Aug. 14, 2017, 9:22 a.m. ET

Zendaya knows how to work a red carpet, and now, she's slaying in a steamy music video, proving she's no longer the teen from her Disney music days.

The Spider-Man actress starred in the sensual video for Bruno Mars's Versace On The Floor, released Sunday, trading in her voluminous curls for sleek straight locks — and looking as mature as ever.

In the video, the two play strangers who are staying in hotel rooms right next door to each other.

Wooed by Mars's sultry vocals and seductive lyrics, the 20-year-old begins dancing around her room until she eventually lets her little, golden dress fall to the floor, perfectly matching the song's title.

Does she ever meet up with Mars in his room next door? That's for you to decide.
