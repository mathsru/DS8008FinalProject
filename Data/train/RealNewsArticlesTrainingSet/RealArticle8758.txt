Lady Gaga Ziplines Upside Down With Boyfriend Christian Carino in Costa Rica: Watch! It's been nearly a year since Lady Gaga performed at the Super Bowl halftime show, but she's still flying high!

During her vacation to Costa Rica, her boyfriend, Christian Carino, shared a video of the pop star ziplining upside down with her arms spread out. "Oh, God! The brake scared the s**t out of me!” Gaga exclaims in the video.

"The Super Bowl jump was only a warm up," Carino captioned the clip on Instagram. "Simply fearless.”

ET confirmed in February that Gaga was dating Carino, her talent agent, and last September, the 31-year-old singer talked with us about getting her boyfriend's input when it comes to her career.

"It's not just about it being my boyfriend," she said of moments when she runs off stage to ask Carino how she did during a performance. "I ask that of many people in my life, and my family, all the time."
