Lots of people opt for a unique term of endearment when referring to their grandmas or grandpas. But they probably aren't using a nickname quite like the one Brandi Glanville calls her grandmother.

The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills alum revealed the eyebrow-raising moniker in a sneak peek of her session with Tyler Henry in the upcoming episode of Hollywood Medium, which you can view over at Us Weekly. The medium relays a message to Brandi from her late grandfather that's meant for her grandma, who is watching from another room, saying that he wants her to take it easy these days. “The way that this comes across is basically saying I’m not worried about health but if there are going to be any issues, she might kind of overdo it by her own doing,” Tyler explained. "It's not like grandma has to worry about her health, she just might have to pace herself.”

That's a sentiment that Brandi shares about her grandma, too. "Yeah, you don't need to garden every day and live by yourself and be so, I call her 'gangster bitch grandma,'" Brandi told Tyler, to which her grandma replied, "Shut up, Brandi."

But you could tell that there was still a lot of love between Brandi and her grandma, no matter what name she calls her.
