The 50 Best Gift Ideas for Women, Just in Time for the Holidays When it comes to holiday shopping for the leading ladies in your circle, the best gifts for women are less about spending big and more about choosing something thoughtful—especially with Christmas mere days away and delivery timing top of mind. The most appreciated picks tend to be small upgrades or personal touches she might not buy for herself, like luxe pajamas or a journal that encourages her to take five minutes to herself.

If your partner, sister, or mom could use some self-care, a weighted sleep mask is a great pick for a Christmas or just-because gift. Looking to go the meaningful route for a best friend or aunt? Choose something that’ll remind her of her loved ones (think a digital frame preloaded with personal photos). And for anyone who’s tough to shop for, reliable standbys like high-end candles, cozy loungewear, and skin care gift sets are hard to miss.

Every gift here has been vetted by Glamour editors who’ve given and received them, and all are options that should arrive in time for Christmas. Add a card, and you’re covered for everyone on your list—without it feeling too last-minute.
