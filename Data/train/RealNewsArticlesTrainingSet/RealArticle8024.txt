Heidi Klum & Tom Kaulitz Make Their Red Carpet Couple Debut at Cannes amfAR Gala Trending on Billboard

The Cannes film festival is drawing to a close, but Heidi Klum still made headlines Friday (May 18) by hitting the red carpet with beau Tom Kaulitz.

Decked in a gorgeous white beaded gown, the America’s Got Talent judge attended the Cannes amFAR gala with the Tokio Hotel musician, marking their first public appearance as a couple. Rumors began to swirl about their relationship back in March, when Klum was seen kissing Kaulitz in pictures published by The Daily Mail.
