Here Are All The New Characters From "13 Reasons Why" Season 2 13 Reasons Why season one was based on a book of the same name. Without a book sequel, season 2 of the acclaimed show was sure to surprise hardcore and new fans alike (and fans who have binge-watched the new season in its entirety are truly shocked).

It should come as no surprise that there's going to be a whole new cast of characters joining the characters you've already come to know, love, AND hate. Here's everything you need to know about the additions...

Chloe is Liberty High’s beautiful “it girl’ who’s also the new head cheerleader. The actress who's playing her, Anne Winters, has appeared in FX’s Tyrant and ABC’s Wicked City.

Getty Images

Chelsea is an artsy, witty type unafraid to speak her mind. Alden appeared on American Horror Story: Roanoke last year.

Mackenzie's brother, Cyrus is an edgy, cynical mischief-maker who champions the downtrodden. This is Cass’s first major role.

Jackie is a warm, intelligent, passionate advocate for victims of bullying. Kelli is a Broadway star who won a Tony for The King and I in 2015. She's also appeared in CBS All Access’s The Good Fight.

Nina is a respected track star with a secret that's bound to cause trouble in season two. Samantha has appeared on MTV’s Teen Wolf and Freeform’s The Fosters among others.



Getty Images

Sonya is a smart and ambitious young litigator. Could she play a role in finally bringing Bryce to justice for raping Jessica? Allison starred in Syfy’s Incorporated and Fox’s Terra Nova.



Getty Images

Ben Lawson as Rick

Lawson plays Liberty High's beloved baseball coach, which means he was probably devastated when Jeff Atkins died in that car crash during season one.



Jake Weber and Brenda Strong as Barry and Nora

Barry and Nora are Bryce's parents. They were leaders in their community, but that could all change once Bryce begins facing the consequences of raping Jess in season one. The actors that play them, Jake and Brenda, are industry vets. Jake starred in NBC's Medium and Brenda played Mary Alice Young on Desperate Housewives.



Getty Images

Meredith Monroe as Carolyn

Carolyn is Alex's mother. She's a hardworking nurse, so could she have been working at the hospital when Alex came in with a gunshot wound? How will she react when she learns how Alex ended up with his injury? Was it self-inflicted or did Tyler shoot him (like some fans are theorizing)? Meredith won't have any trouble in her emotional scenes since she played Andie McPhee in the popular teen drama, Dawson's Creek.

Getty Images

R.J. Brown as Caleb

Caleb is Tony's boxing trainer. He's a fierce competitor inside the ring, but the funniest guy in the room outside of it. You might recognize R.J. Brown because he played Thomas White on The Carrie Diaries.
