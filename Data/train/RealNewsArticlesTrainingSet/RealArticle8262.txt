Emmy Awards 2017: Stephen Colbert posts 'Hot Leak from Russia' hours before hosting Presenter Stephen Colbert, who is just some hours away from hosting this year's Primetime Emmy Awards, has shared an amusing video a before taking the stage at the star studded ceremony

Presenter Stephen Colbert, who is just some hours away from hosting this year's Primetime Emmy Awards, has shared an amusing video a before taking the stage at the star studded ceremony.

Host Stephen Colbert dances onstage during the 69th Emmy Awards at the Microsoft Theatre in Los Angeles. Pic/AFP

The 'Late Show' host tried to keep things controversial by captioning the video, 'hot leak from Russia! Sorry, not the one you've been waiting for.'

The video shows clips from his recent trip to Russia.

In the footage, Colbert can be seen asking people, 'Who should win the Emmy for Outstanding Drama Series?'

All the people replied with the same answers, 'The Americans'.But, 'The Americans' is not even nominated for an Emmy this year.

But as the funny video suggests, 'The Russians have rigged bigger elections than this.'
