Game of Thrones boss David Benioff discusses Jon Snow, Daenerys' complicated attraction- The New Indian Express "There's conflict and it's conflict between powerful people and then to make it all even more complicated, they're starting to be attracted to each other. So much of it is not from dialogue or anything we wrote. It's just the two of them in a small space, standing near each other, and us just watching that and feeling the heat of it," Benioff said.



Talking about Daenerys, co-creator D.B. Weiss added, "She's in a position where if she doesn't step up soon and come up with a big win for her side, she's going to lose this fight before it even begins.”
