Just days after cleaning all her social accounts, Taylor Swift has posted a mysterious video, adding to the speculation that singer is about to make her musical comeback.

On Friday, Taylor deleted all previous posts and her profile pictures from her Instagram, Twitter and Facebook.

Even Taylor’s website was a blank space!

Now she’s posted a 10 second dark video with no audio of what looks like an animal’s tail.

What does it all mean? Another album? We're not sure yet but it's certainly whipped fans into a frenzy!

Taylor has avoided the public eye ever since her spat with Kanye West and break up with British actor Tom Hiddleston.

Swift’s last album was 2014’s ‘1989’, her last single was her ‘I Don’t Wanna Live Forever’ collab with ZAYN in January for the Fifty Shades Darker soundtrack.
