Maroon 5 and Jimmy Fallon gave subway commuters the best surprise Even when Adam Levine is wearing an unrecognisable disguise, he can still draw a crowd.

For a segment on The Tonight Show, Maroon 5 and Jimmy Fallon took to the NYC subway for a spot of busking. They kicked things off with an incognito rendition of "Crazy Little Thing Called Love", before abruptly whipping off their disguises and launching straight into "Sugar".

We imagine the only people who wouldn't have enjoyed the surprise were the subway security team who had to deal with that massive crowd.
