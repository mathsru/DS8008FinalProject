Cena, 40, proposed to Bella, 34, at Wrestlemania 33 in April after dating for five years, and the couple recently got engaged. Bella, her sister Brie and a bunch of other Total Divas are in Mexico to celebrate the engagement. "We're going to a five star restaurant because Nicole [Bella] only likes nice things," Brie said.

World Wrestling Entertainment's Total Diva Nikki Bella is all excited about her upcoming marriage to wrestling super star John Cena.
