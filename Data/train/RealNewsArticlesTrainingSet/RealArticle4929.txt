Selena Gomez Wore 6 Outfits in 1 Day — But She Loved Her Date Night Dress Best Selena Gomez and the Weeknd stepped out for date night on Monday evening in New York City, displaying some serious couple goals by way of coordinating outfits.

The pair dressed in the same black and silver color palette, complimenting one another’s looks head-to-toe. From the simplicity of Gomez’s plunging lace neckline, to the detail on the Weeknd’s jacket, the duo seem to complete each other in more ways than one.

This isn’t the first time that the couple has been recognized for their impeccable taste — may we remind you of their red-carpet debut — but this seems to be one of Gomez’s favorite looks. So much so that she posted three photos on her Instagram in the dress, even though it was only one of six she wore that day.

The date came at the end of a busy press day for Gomez, who managed to pull multiple ensembles in less than 24 hours. But her biggest feat was wearing the same Louis Vuitton heels for all but one of the day’s outfits.
