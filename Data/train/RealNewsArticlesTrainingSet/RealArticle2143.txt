31 Unique Halloween Costume Ideas for 2018 According to the Allure version of the Farmer’s Almanac, autumn begins on September 1. We've already broken out the fall decor, queued up horror movies, and started planning Halloween festivities. We’ve waited all summer for this.

And this spooky season, expect a fresh crop of unique Halloween costumes. You’ll see plenty of pop stars to fit every personality, celebrity couple, and plenty of film- and TV-inspired ways to play dress-up. And if you don't think you're going to see at least one Labubu, you're delulu.

Don’t have time to do it up? We hear you and, of course, you have options, too. There’s absolutely nothing wrong with just popping on some cat ears or wearing an apron and calling it a chef's costume. Ahead, you’ll find the best Halloween costume ideas and beauty products you can use to pull everything together.

Labubu

Was there any bigger pop culture moment more destined to be a Halloween costume this year than Labubus? Children and adults alike became absolutely obsessed with the furry figures and their cute yet sinister smiles, dangling the darlings from their bags, collecting them on dedicated shelves, and even dressing them up in customized clothing. To capture the Labubu look, you can wear a fleecy, hooded onesie with ears like this one we found on Amazon (and repurpose it as cozy pajamas once Halloween is over). There’s no shortage of Labubu makeup inspiration on social media—some scarier than others—but we like this one by content creator Briana. The good news: If it doesn’t turn out as well as you’d like, you can just tell people you’re a Lafufu!
