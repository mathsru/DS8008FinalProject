Taylor Swift announces Charli XCX, Camila Cabello will open on 'Reputation' tour March 1, 2018 Updated March 2, 2018, 8:06 a.m. ET

Taylor Swift Thursday added some singing firepower to her Reputation stadium tour, announcing that Charli XCX and Camila Cabello will perform as opening acts.

The music superstar tweeted the news, along with a short, accompanying video, saying: "I have a very exciting update to share... @Camila_Cabello and @charli_xcx will be the opening acts on the #reputationStadiumTour!!!"

Both singers tweeted their gratitude. Charli XCX said: "I am so excited. Thank U Taylor for having me."

Former Fifth Harmony member Cabello, who released her debut solo album, Camila, in January, tweeted: "On every level, this is a dream come true, thank you Taylor for having me from the bottom of my heart, I love you so much !!!!!!!!"

The first leg of the Reputation tour, which includes 27 North American dates, begins May 8 in Glendale, Ariz. Swift's website has also promised additional tour dates that will be announced for venues in the United Kingdom, Ireland, Australia and New Zealand.

Reputation, Swift's sixth studio album, was released in November.
