Every year, the Veuve Clicquot Polo Classic takes place in New York City, inviting polo-lovers and all-around fashionistas and fashionistos to watch a polo match and enjoy a day of picknicking in Liberty Sate Park. Thanks to social media, the annual event has become a #Blackmoment, where everywhere comes dressed in their Sunday best. And whether you’re there or not, you’ll spend a lot of time just scramming through the gram, getting your life from these looks. And honestly, the flicks are amazing. There’s a bit of church, a dash of brunch, and a whole lot of Black excellence. Hands down, it’s the best place to catch some dope fashion looks while sitting out on the lawn, sipping on your champagne. And I love any event that turns into a Black family reunion! If you need some fashion inspo for the summer, just look at these fire fits spotted this weekend at the Veuve Clicquot Polo Classic sponsored by Hublot.
