Tiffany Haddish Claims Actress ‘On Drugs’ Bit Beyoncé’s Face Tiffany Haddish keeps spilling Queen Bey tea. After claiming that Beyoncé confronted an actress who touched JAY-Z, the Girls Trip actress reveals new details about the bash.

Speaking with GQ about the December party, Haddish claimed that a drugged out actress bit Beyoncé in the face. “She bit Beyoncé in the face,” she says. “Beyoncé stormed away, went up to JAY-Z, and was like, ‘JAY! Come here! This bitch–‘ and snatched him. They went to the back of the room. I was like, ‘What just happened?’ And Beyoncé’s friend walked up and was like, ‘Can you believe this bitch just bit Beyoncé?'”

‘Yoncé was apparently cool about the incident. “Near the end of the party, Beyoncé’s at the bar, so I said to Beyoncé, ‘Did she really bite you?’ She was like, ‘Yeah.’ I was like, ‘She gonna get her ass beat tonight,'” Haddish said. “She was like, ‘Tiffany, no. Don’t do that. That bitch is on drugs. She not even drunk. The bitch is on drugs. She not like that all the time. Just chill.'”

Bey’s longtime rep Yvette Noel-Schure offered a statement in response. “I absolutely cannot comment on any of this, as I have no knowledge,” she said.

‘Yoncé appeared to reference Haddish’s candid party tea on “Top Off,” her DJ Khaled, JAY-Z, and Future collaboration. “If they’re tryna party with the queen,” she rapped, “they gon’ have to sign a non-disclosure.”

Haddish celebrated the song and the lyric. “I Love Beyonce part! Everything she said rang so real to me. Just know I will sign A NDA any day For Beyoncé,” she tweeted. “But also know this I will fight for Beyoncé. I Love that woman she is a Gift from God.”
