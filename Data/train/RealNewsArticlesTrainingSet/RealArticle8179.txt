Non-Taylor-Approved Notes

That the split Swift does on the car actually has history behind it. Swift originally couldn't do a split and explained in a 2016 interview that she worked on the skill every day for a year. “I got it in my head that I couldn’t do a split, and I was really upset about it," she said. "And so I stretched every single day for a year until I could do a split. Somehow I feel better knowing that I can.” On Tumblr, Swift liked several posts applauding her split.
