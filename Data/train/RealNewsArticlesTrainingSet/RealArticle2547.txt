Candice Swanepoel Welcomes Her Second Child Gisele Bündchen gave fans a look at her baby bump for the first-time since her pregnancy news was revealed.

On Instagram, the supermodel shared several photos from the Lotus House Women’s Shelter’s 20th anniversary gala in Miami, Florida, which took place on November 9. The snaps offered a first look at Bündchen’s maternity style, with the fashion icon dressing her bump in a slinky black midi dress with a tied-off halter neckline and a backless design. She styled the look simply, opting for a pair of strappy black heeled sandals and gold hoop earrings.

Other photos show Bündchen volunteering with the organization. In one picture taken inside of a cafeteria, she wears a loose white blouse with mid-length sleeves and a pair of blue jeans. In another, she poses with a helmet and a pink hard hat while wearing a Children’s Village T-shirt, blue skinny jeans, and white sneakers.

“It was an honor to attend the Lotus House Gala celebrating their 20th anniversary as the largest shelter for women and children in America,” Bündchen wrote in the caption. “I feel truly blessed to support the visionary Constance Collins and her incredible team, whose unwavering commitment has transformed countless lives. With their latest achievement—uniting over 300 women’s shelters nationwide through the National Women’s Shelter Network—they’re taking a bold step toward eradicating homelessness. Learn more through the link in my bio and find out how you can help. 🙏 Together, we are stronger!”

Bündchen is expecting her first child with boyfriend Joaquim Valente. She is already a mom to two kids, whom she shares with ex-husband Tom Brady: 14-year-old son Benjamin Rein and 11-year-old daughter Vivian Lake.

“Gisele and Joaquim are happy for this new chapter in their life,” a source recently told People. “They’re looking forward to creating a peaceful and loving environment for the whole family.”
