Serena Williams pens emotional note to her unborn baby Serena Williams is one excited mom-to-be.

The tennis champ posted an emotional note to her unborn baby on Monday morning, revealing deep sentiments about how her pregnancy has taught her a lot about herself.

“My dearest Baby, You gave me the strength I didn’t know I had. You taught me the true meaning of serenity and peace. I can’t wait to meet you. I can’t wait for you to join the players box next year,” she wrote next to a photo of herself in a swimsuit revealing a baby bump.

“But most importantly, I am so happy to share being number one in the world with you…. Once again today. On @alexisohanian bday. from the world’s oldest number one to the world’s youngest number one. – Your mommy.”

Williams, 35, broke the news last week that she was 20 weeks pregnant. She is engaged to Reddit co-founder Alexis Ohanian. It is the couple’s first child.
