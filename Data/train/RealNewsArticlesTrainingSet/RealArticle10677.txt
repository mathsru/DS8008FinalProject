The Roseanne revival has a premiere date The Roseanne revival has a premiere date

As foretold by the vague promise last May of a midseason premiere, ABC today revealed that the nine-episode Roseanne revival will officially debut Tuesday, March 27 at 8 p.m. ET with an hourlong premiere, per Variety. It’ll keep that 8 p.m. time slot the following week, but return to its normal half-hour episodes.
