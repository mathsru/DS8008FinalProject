Pop star Miley Cyrus has paid $5.8 million for a 6,869-square-foot home in Williamson County.

A trust linked to the "Wrecking Ball" singer bought the 33.5-acre property located northwest of downtown Franklin from Keith A. Hays.

“It’s a prominent, sought-after area for those that still would like to be close to either Franklin or Nashville, but seeking a rural, equestrian environment,” said Steve Fridrich, owner of Fridrich & Clark Realty.

The 2014-built home has five bedrooms, four bathrooms and two half baths plus three-car garage and two fireplaces. It is located off Hillsboro Road and Highway 96.
