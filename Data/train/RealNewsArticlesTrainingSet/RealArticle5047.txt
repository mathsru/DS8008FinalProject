Who needs Tinder when Ray Romano is your dad?

The 59-year-old comedian used his latest appearance on Jimmy Kimmel Live to introduce the world to his 24-year-old son, Matt.

Matt just so happens to be single (and fluent in Spanish, in case you were wondering).

He works behind-the-scenes on Jimmy's show -- and was left red-faced after being lured on stage by his famous dad, who proceeded to probe him on his relationship status.

"My wife wants grandchildren, so he needs to pick up the pace there," the Everybody Loves Raymond star said, attempting to justify his nosiness to viewers.

Ray, who shares four children -- Matt, Joseph, Gregory, and Ally -- with his actress wife of 29 years, Anna Romano, was promoting his new mobster comedy series Get Shorty. But the conversation was less about his latest project, and more about his son's love life.

Ray with daughter Alexandra, and sons Matthew, and Gregory in 2001. (Source: Getty)

Of course, the stand-up comic left it to host Jimmy to ask Matt the hard questions about whether he was in fact dating anyone.

"No — not right now," Matt sheepishly answered his boss, who asked him straight up if he was seeing anybody. "No girlfriend."

But Ray wasn't done embarrassing his son just yet.

And if for some reason it doesn't work out with Matt -- Ray even offered a backup plan:

"He has an identical twin, so God forbid should anything happen to him..."
