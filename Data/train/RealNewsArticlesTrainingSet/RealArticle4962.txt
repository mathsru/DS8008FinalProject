Whenever Cara Delevingne’s name is mentioned, the first thing that comes to mind are her bold, iconic brows. She’s inspired many to grow out a thicker, fuller set, and we’ve always wondered what she uses to maintain her own. Well, we’re in luck: Cara has finally shared the one brow product she swears by, and luckily we can all find it at the drugstore.

She told Refinery29 that she relies on Rimmel London’s Brow This Way lightweight gel. It’s available in clear, blonde, medium brown and dark brown—so there’s a perfect shade for all of us. Another plus is that the brush applicator is really easy to use, and it costs only $4. This new development will bring us one step closer to the actress-model’s enviable arches.
