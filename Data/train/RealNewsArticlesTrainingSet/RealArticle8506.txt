Experience Kendall Jenner’s Closet in 360 Degrees Note: If you have an Apple mobile device or viewing this on Safari, the 360 video will look best when viewed through the YouTube App.

Kendall Jenner’s street style has been particularly exciting to watch in the last year. Between her penchant for retro fanny packs and her obsession with statement shoes (thigh-highs, transparent booties—you name it, she’s tried it!), the supermodel has a healthy appetite for the most tricky-to-pull-off trends; in fact, she’s been responsible for setting a few of them in motion. It certainly makes the prospect of peeking inside her closet—with Google Daydream’s 360-degree technology, no less—even more intriguing. The supermodel has not one but two wardrobes inside her apartment in Los Angeles, and invited Vogue in for the grand tour earlier this summer.
