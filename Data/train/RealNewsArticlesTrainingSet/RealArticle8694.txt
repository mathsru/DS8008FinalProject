Matt Damon may say he’s not a fan of Jimmy Kimmel, but thanks to a prank by Ellen DeGeneres, Damon briefly became the biggest fan of a plastic version of the late-night host.

The product in question? An underwear-clad blow-up doll with Kimmel’s face.
