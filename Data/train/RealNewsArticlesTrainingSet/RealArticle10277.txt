In Miami, there’s always a reason to celebrate and Thursday, December 7th was certainly no exception.

Kicking off Art Basel weekend, the Eden Roc Miami Beach Hotel dazzled with celebrities, musicians, artists, philanthropists and other distinguished guests as The Global Gift Gala returned to the Magic City. In a night filled with passionate speeches, star-studded musical performances, a champagne reception and art auction, this charitable gala brought together hundreds of A-listers. Global Gift Foundation Honorary Chairs Eva Longoria Bastón and Ricky Martin lead the event as they came together to raise money for hurricane recovery efforts in struggling Puerto Puerto.

Photo Credit: ExcslusiveAccess.Net

When Hurricane Maria tore through Puerto Rico in September 2017 it brought tremendous devastation to the island. After leaving all of its residents without power, The Global Gift Foundation, an organization committed to fighting poverty, health, education and social injustice, knew it needed to do something to help.

In response, it teamed up with the charitable organizations of its Honorary Chairs, the Eva Longoria Foundation and Ricky Martin Foundation, to host a star-studded gala that would raise money for a part of the United States that desperately needed our help.

Photo Credit: ExcslusiveAccess.Net

A cocktail reception kicked off this sold-out event and glasses clinked as Miami’s most beautiful swirled around the Eden Roc Miami Beach Hotel ballroom donning the season’s most striking gowns. A-list guests — like Serena Williams, Adrienne Bailon, Domingo Zapata and Zuleyka Rivera, to name a few—walked the red carpet and grabbed their seats. The room was filled to the brim with partygoers who indulged under a magical setting of glittering chandeliers, deep purple mood lighting and fairytale-like flowers in blush pinks, reds and corals.

Photo Credit: ExcslusiveAccess.Net

As waiters spun around carrying trays of porcini-crusted Angus beef tenderloin, salty caramel chocolate tart and mango coconut panna cotta, guests were treated to surprise musical performances from Ednita Nazario, Adrienne Bailon and Israel Houghton, Oskar Cartaya and Jeimy Osorio.

MSNBC’s Mariana Atencio emceed the event, and a live art auction featured pieces by standout artists, including Domingo Zapata and Alexander Mijares. The profits raised by the auction went directly to the victims of Hurricane Maria through The Global Gift Foundation.

Photo Credit: ExcslusiveAccess.Net

Making a point to honor those who have been making a difference already, Eva Longoria Bastón took a moment to present international recording artist and philanthropist, Miguel Bosé, with the Global Gift Foundation Humanitarian Award. This award recognizes those individuals “who have distinguished themselves through exceptional philanthropic service and activism.”

Photo Credit: ExcslusiveAccess.Net

Demonstrating their commitment to the Puerto Rican people, Eva Longoria Bastón and Ricky Martin, longtime advocates for the Latin community, gave passionate speeches about their love for Puerto Rico, first hand accounts about the state of the island in the aftermath of Hurricane Maria, and most importantly their dedication to restoring its beauty and spirit.

“I am angry, and I won’t stop talking about it,” said Ricky Martin, Grammy Award-winning singer and founder of the Ricky Martin Foundation. “Puerto Rico is my home, and they need our help.”

Having raised $50K+ in ticket sales alone during last night’s stunning event, the Global Gift Foundation promises the residents of Puerto Rico one thing for sure: help is on the way.
