Mel C Says the Spice Girls Were Never Asked to Perform at the Royal Wedding Stop right now! In case you missed it, the Spice Girls had a surprise reunion in London on Saturday, in honor of Victoria Beckham (a.k.a. Posh Spice) on her 50th birthday.

Along with Beckham, the four other members of the group were present, including Mel B (Scary Spice), Melanie C (Sporty Spice), Emma Bunton (Baby Spice), and Geri Halliwell (Ginger Spice).

The moment was documented on Beckham’s Instagram account, where the designer included a pic with her former bandmates and wrote, “The best gift to be reunited!! Thank you to all my friends and family for celebrating with me. Kisses! xxx.”

On husband David’s Instagram, we also got a glimpse of the group’s impromptu performance of their hit 1998 single “Stop.” In a video shared by the retired pro footballer, the group sings along to the track in front of A-list attendees including Tom Cruise, Eva Longoria, and Gordon Ramsay.

For the celebratory evening, Beckham chose a dress from her own line’s Spring/Summer ’24 collection—a sheer seafoam gown that featured asymmetrical arm holes and gathered fabric around the sides, as well as ruched seams that looked like flowers. Underneath the dress, Beckham wore a black bra-and-panties set, and she accessorized with a thick silver cuff.

Heading into the ceremony, the birthday girl was seen coming in on crutches (she broke her foot while working out in February), and on her way out, her hubby actually carried her on his back. The fashion designer enjoyed the support of not only her friends, but also her family, as all four of her kids— Brooklyn, 25, Romeo, 21, Cruz, 19, and Harper, 12—were present.

The fashion designer gushed in a different Instagram post later, writing, “I don’t think I’ve ever felt as loved as I did last night. Thank you all for coming from near and so far!! X.”
