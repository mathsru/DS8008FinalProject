Joe Alwyn References, T Swift Reputation Lyrics Taylor Swift just released "Gorgeous," the third single from her album Reputation. And, Swift's fans claim she told them it's about her boyfriend Joe Alwyn during her Reputation Secret Sessions with them.

The lyrics describe how they met and she fell for him...when she was still dating someone else (Tom Hiddleston, perhaps?)

But the words, always open to interpretation, really speak for themselves. Here they are:

Swift's last boyfriend before 26-year-old actor Joe Alwyn was 36-year-old actor Tom Hiddleston. Swift and Hiddleston officially broke up in September 2016 (though a reconciliation rumor went around at the end of October last year.) While it's unclear when Swift and Alwyn officially first met and started dating, they were filmed leaving a private Kings of Leon concert together in October 2016. Swift went to the screening of Alwyn's film Billy Lynn's Long Halftime Walk in November.
