‘13 Reasons Why’ actor Uriah Shelton hit with restraining order after allegedly injuring pal with a kick to the stomach “Girl Meets World” and “13 Reasons Why” actor Uriah Shelton was hit with a restraining order after a former pal says he kicked her so hard it caused serious injury.

Shelton, 20, allegedly kicked the unidentified woman in the stomach after an argument arose when she tried to stop him from getting into his car, according to documents obtained by People.

“I was standing in the way of his truck door (and) he kicked me in the stomach to get me out of the way,” wrote the woman, who described herself as his former best friend and a previous sexual partner.

She wrote that, because he is a trained MMA fighter, his blow caused “abdominal and chest trauma, internal muscle bruising, (and) inflamed intestines.”

The woman also alleged that Shelton threatened her with talk of firearms.

A rep for the actor — who was reportedly ordered by a judge to stay 100 yards away from the woman — told E! News that he would “never harm another person.”

Shelton’s lawyer had no comment when reached by the Daily News.

The young actor had a minor role on the popular Netflix drama “13 Reasons Why” as Pratters, a classmate of the main character Hannah.

He also appeared in eight episodes of the Disney Channel series “Girl Meets World” as Josh Matthews, the uncle of main character Riley (daughter of Ben Savage’s Corey).

Shelton previously revealed he and co-star Rowan Blanchard had beef.

“Rowan doesn’t necessarily like me,” he told M Magazine in May. “We’ve had a lot of (differences). I don’t care.”
