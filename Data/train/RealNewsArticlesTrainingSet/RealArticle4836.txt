Pippa Middleton 's wedding to suave James Matthews is set to be a rather lavish affair - with even the outside toilets being posh.

‌



It is estimated the wedding will cost 15 times more than the average big day - despite the power couple opting to have their reception at Pippa Middleton's £6million family home.

‌



The Middleton's have shelled out £4,000 for "luxury" outdoor toilets complete with fresh linen and posh soaps.

‌



‌



1. Venue/Marquee – £65,000

Although the reception is being hosted at the Middleton's home, they are still expecting 350 guests, meaning a huge - and deluxe - marquee is required.

Pippa is very close to her family and her parents will be hosting the reception (Image: Sunday Mirror)

This, Bridebook estimates, will start at £25,000 but can easily go much higher.

‌



Even though it's May, that's no guarantee of good weather either.

So it may be there's another marquee for drinks in case it rains, plus a fully-equipped catering tent.

2. Food – £70,000

Pippa and James are used to a certain lifestyle, and no doubt will want to feed and water their guests well.

‌



A tip-top London caterer will charge over £170 per guest for catering.

A food enthusiast, Pippa is bound to put on a good spread

This includes all food, chefs and staffing, glassware and crockery hire, and linen hire.

Because their reception is in a marquee, an extra level of staffing is required, increasing Pippa's catering costs to around £200 per guest, of which she's inviting 350.
