The red carpet isn't the time to play it safe when it comes to fashion. So, it's no surprise that Jenna Dewan-Tatum took a risk at this year's American Music Awards, leaving very little to the imagination...

YAAAS QUEEN! The actress/dancer wore this gorgeous gold Julien Macdonald gown and looked absolutely stunning.

"@jennadewan looked every inch the smouldering superstar at last night’s @amas wearing a sparkly @Swarovski crystal embellished plunge-neck #JulienMacdonald gown that had every head turning," designer Julien Macdonald wrote on Instagram.

Macdonald wasn't the only one who praised Dewan-Tatum for her amazing red carpet fashion. She made several Best Dressed lists, including E! News' red carpet round-up and Vogue's compilation of best dressed celebs.
