Marc Anthony says he was forced to build up his confidence after his father told him he was ugly as a child.

The 45-year-old claims in a new interview that his dad urged him as a youngster to 'work on your personality' because he deemed himself and his son as unattractive.

And now the hitmaking pop star credits his father for his own success, explaining that it taught him to not ' rely on my looks to stand out'.

Speaking to Men's Fitness, Anthony recounted: 'My dad would tell me, "Son, I’m ugly and you’re ugly - work on your personality."'

'I swear to God. I told him a couple of months ago, I said, "Dad, you remember telling me that when I was little?"' The actor and singer said.'He’s like, "I’ll tell you that shit today, too!"'

Adding thoughtfully: 'I think it served me well. I think confidence is a powerful thing.'
