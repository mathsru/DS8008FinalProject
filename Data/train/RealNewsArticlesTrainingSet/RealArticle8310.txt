Britney Spears and boyfriend Sam Asghari celebrate her final Vegas show Updated Jan. 2, 2018, 3:41 p.m. ET

Britney Spears is ending her four-year residency in Las Vegas — and getting lots of love from her boyfriend Sam Asghari.

Asghari posted a video Monday of the Toxic singer performing the last show of her Pieces of Me tour at Planet Hollywood Resort & Casino along with a supportive caption.

"4 years of legacy, 4 years of greatness, a change in entertainment for the better!" he wrote on Instagram. "Extremely proud of my baby for kicking (butt) again tonight, but this time to end #pieceofme In Vegas after 4 years of residency. What a night."

He later posted an adorable video of the two of them with the caption, "Happy New Year from me and my partner in crime."

"You look beautiful," he tells Spears in the video.
