Jennifer Lawrence thanks Kardashians for getting her through 'Mother's intense scenes It seems like the Kardashians have finally helped someone in Hollywood.

While walking the red carpet, Jennifer dished about how much she leaned on 'Keeping Up With the Kardashians' and her "Kardashian Tent," while filming the psychological thriller, reports E! Online.

At Sunday's premiere, Jennifer reiterated her love for the Kardashains, explaining, "It got to a point in the movie where it got so dark I was like ya know what? I need the Kardashians. I'm going to call the Kardashians in for this one."

She previously told the Vogue, "I can give one solid hour of my life away for the Kardashians."

The actress confessed it was talking to ' Black Swan' director that first sold her on doing the film.

She shared, "Before I read a script, when he just pitched my the ideas that were floating around his head they were just so insane, just huge, massive, biblical, in the universe. I'd never heard anything like it."

Helmed by Darren Aronofsky, ' Mother!' stars Jennifer Lawrence, Javier Bardem, Ed Harris and Michelle Pfeiffer in pivotal roles.

The flick is slated to release on 15 September.
