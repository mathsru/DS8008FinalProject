Is Asa Soltan Rahmati Ready for More Kids Already? Asa Soltan Rahmati welcomed her first child into the world back in January, but that doesn't mean her baby fever has cooled down. In fact, the Shahs of Sunset mom is open to expanding her family a little more in the future.

"I’m raising my little family, and we want to definitely give Soltan a brother or a sister," she recently told Parade, noting that she is open to traveling the globe with her family -- parents, brother, and baby daddy, Jermaine Jackson Jr., and all.

Since welcoming Soltan into her life, Asa admits that things are a little different from her days B.B. (before baby). "It’s completely and absolutely changed my life. I just don’t feel like the same person anymore. I don’t remember life before my son, Soltan. He’s five-and-a-half months and I don’t remember what things were like beforehand," she said. "It’s the most beautiful thing. Jermaine and I just sit there when he’s asleep, and we just stare and gush like, 'Oh my God, that’s my chin, and that’s his nose and that’s his eyes.' He has a big Persian Afro of big curls and thick eyebrows and lashes. We just die over him! But I’ll tell you, it’s way harder than I thought too. The working mom thing is so tough. I think mothers are angels, and I couldn’t do it without my own mom because she helps me with him."
