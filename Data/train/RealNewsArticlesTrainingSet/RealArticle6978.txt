Kendall Jenner Ditched Her Signature Choker for This Accessory Has Kendall Jenner just found a new must-have accessory? We think so! For grandma MJ’s birthday bash in San Diego, her go-to ‘90s choker was noticeably absent. Instead, she tied a bandana around her neck to accessorize her off-shoulder top. The look gave off a casual cool vibe, perfect for their low-key family gathering. While it’s not a huge diversion from her traditional go-to, we’re loving this casual take on the trend, and the good news is you probably own a bandana already.
