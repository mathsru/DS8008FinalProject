A Destiny’s Child Reunion at Coachella? The Beyhive Is Buzzing The Beyhive is buzzing about the possibility of a Destiny's Child reunion next year at Coachella.

Beyoncé was initially scheduled to perform at last year's festival, but... well, twins. She was rescheduled to be a headliner this year, and now, fans are speculating that her set could actually be a Destiny's Child reunion.

So where is the speculation stemming from? The original members themselves. Bey, Kelly Rowland, Michelle Williams, LeToya Luckett, and LaTavia Roberson have all hinted that something is in the works. Separately, they've all posted what fans believe are clues on social media—from Beyoncé rocking an AC/DC shirt, but only showing the "DC" letters on IG, to an Instagram story made by Michelle Williams and two posts made by LaTavia Roberson and LeToya Luckett.

Considering that Bey has a history of embedding clues on social media signaling major news, including the arrival of Lemonade and her twins, Rumi and Sir Carter, fans may be on to something. Add that to the fact that next year marks the 20th anniversary of DC's debut, self-titled album, and the plot definitely thickens.
