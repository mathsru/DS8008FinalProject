Watch Ozuna & Cardi B Perform 'La Modelo' at the 2018 Billboard Latin Music Awards Two of Latin‘s hottest superstars came together to give fans an epic performance. Ozuna and Cardi B performed “La Modelo” for the first time on live television Thursday night (April 26) at the BBLMAs. During the Latin Music conference earlier this week, Ozuna shared the story of how he and Cardi B met, explaining he couldn’t believe she wanted to work with him.
