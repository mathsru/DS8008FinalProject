James Corden makes a feminist speech at the GLAMOUR Awards Last night, James Corden picked up Man of the Year at the 2017 GLAMOUR Awards.

Getty Images

He was presented with the award by Liam Payne, who revealed some very funny stories about his long-time friend.

During his acceptance speech, James thanked GLAMOUR for being the first magazine to interview him and publish his work. The 2010 GLAMOUR Awards was also his first ever presenting gig - the start of an incredible career move which has seen him crack America as the host of The Late Late Show.

"I was thinking today that when I hosted this thing, it was the first thing I'd ever hosted in my life. And I remember thinking afterwards, 'I rather enjoyed that', and now that is part of my life and I'm very, very honoured to be back here."
