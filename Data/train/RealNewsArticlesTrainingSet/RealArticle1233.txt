David Letterman's New Netflix Show Has Some Stellar Guests Lined Up Good news: David Letterman is about to get a hell of a lot richer, which is to say: David Letterman is getting a new TV show. My Next Guest Needs No Introduction will be a six-episode limited series which, unlike most shows within the Netflix purview, will be released one at a time, monthly, rather than all at once. Here's a teaser.

Letterman's been off TV since his widely-publicized retirement in May 2015, which was overshadowed by his even-more-publicized retirement beard. Yeah, it's only been two and a half years. But that's a long time in TV.

A good crop of guests make up the first season: George Clooney, Malala Yousafzai, Jay-Z, Tina Fey, Howard Stern, and freakin' Barack Obama. The first episode is set to hit Netflix on January 12.
