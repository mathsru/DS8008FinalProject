To get even coverage, you should exfoliate before applying a self-tanner.

For best results, you want to apply a self-tanner to moist skin.

When applying a self-tanner, you want to use a circular motion.

For a natural look, avoid applying self-tanner to your elbows, knees, and ankles.

After applying self-tanner, you need to give your skin at least 10 minutes to dry before getting dressed.

If the self-tanner you apply has an SPF, you still need to use sunscreen every day.
