Justin Bieber Wears Purpose Tour Merch After Cancelling Tour It’s been about a week since Justin Bieber abruptly canceled his Purpose Tour, citing a need to relax. On top of understandable disappointment, most fans offered support and well wishes to their favorite singer, hoping that he gets the rest that he needs. But this week, when Justin was seen out in Los Angeles wearing the very Purpose Tour merch that fans were hoping to get at his upcoming shows, they didn't respond kindly. One fan account put it bluntly, “stop wearing purpose tour merch. it's kind of a huge slap on our faces.”

Now, it’s likely that Justin has plenty of Purpose Tour merch tees (it was his tour after all) and maybe he just needed a clean tee to step outside in. One person said it perfectly when they wrote, “someone has to wear the remaining shirts.”

Last week, Justin told his fans that he’s been on tour for the last two years and it was time for him to stop and regroup. He seemed genuinely apologetic — but firm about prioritizing his mental health — and it seems highly unlikely that he would follow that statement by taunting fans with a Purpose Tour outfit. Justin has yet to make any follow-up statements about a future tour or concert series. In the meantime, we hope he’s doing well and getting the rest he needs.
