ICYMI, Pippa Middleton and James Matthews are currently enjoying their time as newlyweds right here in Aus.

After sunning on a private island in French Polynesia, the couple touched down in Sydney, enjoying the sights and a Sydney Harbor Bridge Climb.

They then headed north, stopping in Darwin and now for some glamping in WA.

And while their itinerary has been nothing short of impressive, it’s been Pippa’s honeymoon outfits that have been giving us the real envy.

After a relaxing few days of glamping, Pippa departs Perth wearing a pale blue shirt dress by French designer Maje. She paired the poplin dress with her go-to Castaner wedges and SensiStudio bucket bag. So chic.

Jetting into Darwin on a private plane, the 33-year-old sported an aztec print shirt dress teamed with tan sandals, a matching belt and SensiStudio bucket bag.

After climbing the Sydney Harbour Bridge, Pippa and James enjoyed dinner at waterfront restaurant Flying Fish.

True to Middleton form, she wore a Kate Spade floral tie mini dress – which has unfortunately sold out.

Keeping things casual, Middleton opted for a Orla Kiely blouse with skinny jeans and her SensiStudio basket bag.

For footwear, she opted for the perfect holiday shoe – lace-up espadrilles. These ones are the ‘Carina’ canvas style from Castaner.

For a day of exploring in Sydney, Pippa kicked things off in a printed Kate Spade dress, accented with a waist clinching belt and tassel adorned necktie. The flattering cut is perfect on Middleton, who styled the fit-and-flare with Persol sunglasses and a chain strap.
