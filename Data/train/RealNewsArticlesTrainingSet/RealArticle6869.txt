Nicole Richie and Tina Fey Spoof Taylor Swift's "Bad Blood" Video on 'Great News' Thursday night’s new episode of NBC sitcom Great News will involve a parody of Taylor Swift‘s star-studded 2015 “Bad Blood” music video courtesy of the show’s stars, Nicole Richie and Tina Fey.

In a preview clip, Diana St. Tropez (Fey) is at her desk in a swanky office when a headline appears on her computer: “SHOTS FIRED! Portia Scott Griffith DECLARES SQUAD FEUD WITH MMN BOARDROOM BITCH.”

News anchor Portia Scott-Griffith (Richie) is then shown in the all-leather black ensemble that Swift sported in the original music video. “You better wash dish, bish,” she sings. “I’ve had enough of your ish, bish / I’m a famous and rich bish / So you better watch dish, bish.”

She then literally washes a dish while joined by other members of her news team “squad” rapping along. At the end of the clip, it’s revealed that St. Tropez has been watching the same clip at her computer. “Not today,” she says.

The full episode will air Thursday, Oct. 5, on NBC beginning at 9:30 p.m. Eastern.
