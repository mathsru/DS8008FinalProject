Nick and Joe Jonas Went on a Double Date with Priyanka Chopra and Sophie Turner The Jonas Brothers are back together . . . at least for a dinner date. Nick and Joe had a double date with their significant others, Priyanka Chopra and Sophie Turner, in London on Monday. Older brother Kevin, despite being the only married one, was also there, solo as the fifth wheel.

Nick Jonas and Priyanka Chopra have been getting more serious about their relationship recently, even taking a trip to India together where he reportedly met some of her friends and family. Middle bro Joe has already taken the next step with Turner, proposing to the Game of Thrones actress last year.

As for Kevin, he’s been busy with his two kids and wife, former hair stylist Danielle Jonas, who he married in 2009. The couple met while he was still in the Jonas Brothers back in 2007 while on vacation in the Bahamas. They even briefly dabbled in reality television with their E! show, Married to Jonas.

Danielle’s absence doesn’t seem to be cause for concern though. Just last week Kevin was gushing over his wife on Instagram, writing, “Love every moment with you.”
