Melissa Joan Hart slammed for complaining that Hurricane Maria ruined her vacation plans Actress Melissa Joan Hart is being criticized for an Instagram post she shared during the aftermath of the deadly Hurricane Maria.

Hart, 41, apparently complained that Hurricane Maria ruined her family’s vacation plans by sharing a screenshot of a weather alert she received notifying her of the travel changes caused by the Category 5 storm.
