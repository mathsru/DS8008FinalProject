Watch Beyonce Wish Rumi & Sir Carter Happy Birthday At On The Run II Tour Can you believe that it's already been one whole year since Sir and Rumi Carter's births?! Their mother, Beyoncé, wished the baby twins a happy birthday while onstage at the On The Run II Tour with husband JAY-Z.

Beyoncé welcomed the two new additions to her and Hov's family last year. However, the public still hasn't seen the babies' faces. We thought that Bey showed the twins onscreen at an On The Run II Tour concert last week, as words like “LOVE IS UNIVERSAL” and “LOVE NEVER CHANGES” appeared on a mega screen. However, a rep for the star confirmed that it wasn't them.

A source also added to PEOPLE: "At the end of the show the Carter babies were included in a family collage. [However] the babies in the film clip at the beginning were not their children."

During another concert on Wednesday (June 13) night, Beyoncé decided to wish her two babies a very happy birthday mid-way through a performance. Bey was singing “Young Forever” at the Etihad Stadium in Manchester, England, as JAY-Z smiled and looked on. Then, the mommy said: “Happy birthday to Sir and Rumi. We love you.” The audience cheered at the sweet moment and honestly, so did we.
