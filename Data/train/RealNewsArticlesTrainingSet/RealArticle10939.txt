Sara Haines heading back to ‘The View’ after maternity leave Sara Haines is returning to “The View” on Monday, roughly 11 weeks after giving birth to her second child.

During Wednesday’s episode, the talk show announced, “Sara Haines had baby number 2. Check! Looked stunning on the Oscars red carpet. Check! And this Monday, looks who’s about to check box number 3 … Well, good news, people. This Monday, Sara’s back in the chair. Welcome back, Sara.”

Haines, 40, and husband Max Shifrin welcomed daughter Sandra Grace at the end of December. She has been on maternity leave since then. The couple also shares a young son named Alec.

Haines was at Sunday’s Academy Awards covering the red carpet for ABC, which aired the show.
