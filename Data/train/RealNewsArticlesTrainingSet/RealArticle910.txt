Miranda Kerr Just Gave Birth to Her First Child with Evan Spiegel Miranda Kerr is now a mom of four.

The veteran Victoria’s Secret model announced today on Instagram that she has welcomed her fourth son, baby Pierre.

Kerr is also mom to boys Hart, five, and Myles, four, with husband Evan Spiegel, CEO of Snap Inc. She also shares son Flynn, 12, with ex-husband Orlando Bloom.

(Kerr and Bloom divorced in 2013, and the model married Spiegel in 2017. Bloom is now engaged to Katy Perry, and the two welcomed their first child, Daisy Dove Bloom, in August 2020.)

Jeff Kravitz // Getty Images

In her Instagram announcement, Kerr shared a photo of a bouquet of yellow flowers on a table next to a light blue blanket embroidered with the name Pierre, plus a pair of teeny-tiny yellow Louis Vuitton monogram socks.

“We are overjoyed by the arrival of our little ray of sunshine, Pierre Kerr Spiegel. We couldn’t be more excited to welcome our fourth son into our family. Feeling so very blessed 🙏👼🌈🌻,” she captioned the post.

Kerr announced on Snapchat in September that she was pregnant with her fourth child. She revealed her baby bump in a Reformation x Christy Dawn crop top and jeans.

“So excited to announce baby no. 4,” she wrote over the first shot. She added in another: “And it’s a boy.”

In July 2022, before welcoming Pierre, Kerr revealed in an interview that she had always wanted to be a boy mom. “I just love being a mother and I always wanted three boys,” she said, “so I feel really blessed that I have three healthy boys.”
