Khloé Kardashian Reveals Her Biggest Parenting Fear Khloé Kardashian has kept quiet about her private life since welcoming her second child, a son, with ex-boyfriend Tristan Thompson—until now.

In a new teaser clip from the upcoming season of the family's Hulu show, The Kardashians, Kardashian breaks down in tears while talking about the then-upcoming arrival of her baby boy amid her split from Thompson.

"There is something that I’m ready to talk about," she says in the video. "Tristan and I are having another baby, and it’s supposed to be a really exciting time and it’s just a different experience."

"It’s hard to watch her in pain," her mom, Kris Jenner, says through tears.

Days before news broke that Kardashian and Thompson were expecting their second child together via surrogate, reports revealed that the NBA star had fathered a child with a personal trainer with whom he had an affair. The relationship took place while he and Kardashian were trying to rekindle their romance following various infidelity scandals, with Thompson claiming he had changed and wanted to make things work with the Good American co-founder.

"This has been a difficult time in my life, but it’s the start of something positive and happy and beautiful," Kardashian adds in the clip.

Kardashian and Thompson's son was born this August. The two are also parents to daughter True Thompson, 4, and the basketball player is now also dad to son Prince, 5, with ex-girlfriend Jordan Craig, and son Theo, 9 months, with personal trainer Maralee Nichols.
