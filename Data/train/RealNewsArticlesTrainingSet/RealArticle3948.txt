Updated Sept. 20, 2017, 12:04 p.m. ET

Aaron Paul and his wife, Lauren Paul, shared some happy news on Instagram Tuesday.

The Breaking Bad actor announced that the couple is expecting their first child with a photo of his wife cradling her baby bump.

"Look what I did," Paul captioned the photo. "Words can't express how excited I am that this little one has entered our lives. Just thought it was time I would share this beautiful news with all of you."

Lauren Paul also got in on the Instagram fun, sharing her own post that featured an ultrasound of their little one.

The couple wed in 2013.

Paul currently stars in Hulu series The Path, in which he plays a cult member struggling with faith.

"If you have any sort of doubt, you’re a nonbeliever, and are considered weak and thrown out to the curb by the ones you love," Paul told USA TODAY last year.

After Breaking Bad ended, Paul also acted in movies like Triple 9 and Eye in the Sky. As for his current role? He almost turned it down.

"I wasn't interested in jumping into another show just yet. I was a little spoiled on the last one I was a part of," Paul said.
