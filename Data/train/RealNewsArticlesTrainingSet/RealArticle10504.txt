Becca Kufrin Meets Her Suitors in a New Trailer for Season 14 of ‘The Bachelorette’ We’re just two weeks away from welcoming Becca Kufrin back into our living rooms for season 14 of The Bachelorette after witnessing her heartbreaking split from her ex-fiancé, Arie Luyendyk Jr., on season 22 of The Bachelor. The publicist has reportedly gotten over their televised ordeal, even ripping up his paper heart in the first teaser for the show, and now, she’s ready to meet her bachelors — and so are we! Luckily, ABC has given us a sneak peek at their limo arrivals with a brand-new trailer — and let’s just say, these guys are pulling out all the stops.

If you’ll recall, one of her early suitors rode in on a horse for a surprise meet and greet during The Bachelor season finale. Now, one of the brunette beauty’s 28 contestants is taking things up a notch, riding in on a bull. Yes, a bull. (Because limos are SO 2017.)

When the limo finally does arrive, she’s greeted by none other than Arie Luyendyk Jr. himself — er, rather, a cardboard cutout of him. Another of her would-be love interests brought the prop along for their very first encounter. Yikes!

Perhaps most surprising of all, however, is the guy who appears in full chicken costume, complete with yellow fur, a giant beak, and red comb on his head. Flapping his wings and making chicken sounds, he goes for the ultimate dad joke, turning his clucking sounds from “Ba-caw!” to “Becca!”

“What is happening?!” our Bachelorette asks in disbelief. We don’t even know, Becca. We don’t even know. Looks like this one’s gonna be a doozy…
