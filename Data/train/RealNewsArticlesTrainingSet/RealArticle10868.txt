Bella Thorne laughs off fake masturbation video circulating online Bella Thorne is unbothered.

After learning that a video allegedly showing the 19-year-old masturbating had been making its rounds online, Thorne shut the potential scandal down.

In a series of sex-positive tweets, she confirmed that the footage was 100% fake. Thorne kept it real with her 6.66 million followers telling them, “I don’t even masturbate like that.”

The NSFW footage is thought to have been created by splicing snippets from Thorne’s Snapchat videos with clips from an adult film. Thorne is shown talking while riding in her car.

Audio from her Snapchat video is muted and dubbed over with someone talking about her “need for a father.”
