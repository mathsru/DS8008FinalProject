Stephanie Pratt has claimed that ex-boyfriend Jonny Mitchell cheated on her, attempted to pay off those who wanted to tell Steph the true story, and later attacked her.

Taking to Twitter on Thursday, Steph shared screengrabs of messages she received which claimed Jonny slept with two girls when he visited Exeter University to do club promotion, and a second screengrab of a picture of Jonny with a mystery woman.

She also revealed that she called the police after he allegedly ‘attacked’ her.

Now a close friend of Steph’s has told Metro.co.uk that friends of the reality star ‘are really concerned’ and that she is ‘very upset’ and ‘is not talking to any friends’.

They also revealed that they all thought her romance with the former Love Island hopeful was ‘the real deal’.

Metro.co.uk has contacted reps for Jonny for comment.

Steph’s heartache comes days after Jonny’s reps confirmed their split, and released a statement claiming that the Love Island star has ‘fond memories’ of their time together and even that he sends his best wishes for her future.

Stephanie and Jonny started dating in the summer after his Love Island flop and he even was around when she became an aunt to Heidi and Spencer’s baby. A representative for Jonny said: ‘Jonny Mitchell and Stephanie Pratt have ended their relationship. Jonny has fond memories of the times they spent together and wishes Stephanie all the best of luck in the future.’
