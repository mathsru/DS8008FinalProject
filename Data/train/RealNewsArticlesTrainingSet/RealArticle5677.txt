Star-Studded 2017 iHeartRadio Jingle Ball Tour Lineup Revealed How can you get tickets to the 2017 iHeartRadio Jingle Ball Tour, you ask? If you're a Capital One cardholder, you'll be first to get access to high demand tickets through a special iHeartRadio Jingle Ball Capital One Cardholder Pre-Sale in each city. The iHeartRadio Jingle Ball Capital One Cardholder Pre-Sale begins October 11th at 10am local market time and runs through October 14th at 10am local market time or while supplies last. Tickets will be available at www.iHeartRadio.com/CapitalOne.

All other tickets go on sale to the general public on October 16th at 12pm local market time, and will be available at www.iHeartRadio.com/JingleBall.

In addition to the Capital One Cardholder pre-sale, at each of the tour stops, one lucky Capital One cardholder will have the exclusive opportunity to announce an artist on stage. To learn more visit www.iHeartRadio.com/CapitalOne beginning in November for more details.
