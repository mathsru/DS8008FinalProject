Inside Kourtney Kardashian's Romantic Egyptian Getaway Keeping up with Kourtney Kardashian is always a whirlwind, especially given her penchant for wanderlust. Her most recent adventure? A drool-worthy vacation to Egypt.

Kardashian and her boyfriend, Younes Bendjima, jetted off to the North African nation for a getaway with friends, People reports.

According to People, the couple arrived in Cairo on Sunday along with friends, including Kardashian pal Simon Huck, and a security team. The group has been spending their vacation doing all of the typical tourist activities—like riding camels, seeing the pyramids, and posing in front of the Great Sphinx—as well as living it up at Hacienda Bay and on the northern coast beaches.

In one postcard-worthy photo shared to Huck's Instagram account, Kardashian and her group are seen riding camels through the desert. "RAIDERS OF THE LOST ARC," he captioned the stunning shot. For her part, Kardashian took to Snapchat to share a video at the foursome's attempt at smoking a traditional hookah, as well as to show off some of her favorite meals from the trip thus far, including a colorful fruit plate and a delicious-looking bowl of pasta.
