Ryan Phillippe hospitalized with leg injury: 'I'm going to be ok' Ouch!

Ryan Phillippe did not have a relaxing weekend, having spent his Sunday in a California hospital with a leg injury.

“I’m going to be ok & I appreciate your concern,” he captioned a picture of himself giving a thumbs up while laid up in a hospital bed.

The 42-year-old posted this photo on Instagram on Sunday.

The “Cruel Intentions” star also posted a photo of his bandaged leg to Instagram Stories with caption “not lit.”

Earlier, Phillippe had shared a snap of a drink beside his right hand covered in knuckle Band-Aids.

The actor performs all his own stunts on the television show “Shooter.”

It’s not clear if the 42-year-old actor sustained his injuries on the set of the USA Network show “Shooter,” in which he plays exiled Marine marksman Bobby Lee Swagger.

“It’s a tough show to shoot. The last two weeks we’ve been in the desert, Palmdale, you know, in degrees over 110. And it’s a physical show, and I do all of my own stunts,” he told PopSugar recently.

“We kind of really push it to the limits. But I love it at the same time. It’s exhausting. I’ve got bruises,” he continued.
