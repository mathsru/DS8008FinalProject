Kim Kardashian has revealed the weirdest habit Kanye West has.

The 'Keeping Up With The Kardashians' star, who married 'Ye in 2014, appeared on a recent episode of Jimmy Kimmel which was being guest hosted by Hollywood actress Jennifer Laurence.

The Oscar-winner grilled Kardashian with a stream of personal questions, sharing laughs as they discussed farting, stalking exes and drunk dinner dates with Kris Jenner.

When asked to reveal the weirdest thing Kanye does, Kim didn't hesitate to answer.

"He falls asleep anywhere," she explained.

"We'll be at a meeting or he'll introduce me to people I've never met before, we'll be at a restaurant and he'll be snoring at the table."

The star also revealed how the famous couple like to unwind at the end of the day.

"We are super normal. We watch 'Family Feud' every night before we fall asleep."

During the interview, Jennifer quizzed Kim on her husband's discography, asking her how many of the rapper's albums she could name.

"All of them," she replied, confidently.
