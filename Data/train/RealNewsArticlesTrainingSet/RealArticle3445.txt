Kim Kardashian Shares an Adorable Home Video For North West's Birthday They grow up so fast! It’s officially North West’s last year as a preteen.

On June 15, Kim Kardashian shared a special tribute for her daughter—who she shares with ex-husband Kanye West—in honor of her 12th birthday.

In a large carousel of photos, the Skims founder took to Instagram to share never-before-seen snaps from North’s early years.

The intimate collection features Kim kissing baby North’s cheek in one sweet image, followed by multiple snapshots of her as a toddler.

She captioned the post, “My little baby North turns 12 years old today. We were looking at pics today together and laughing so hard, you were so small.”

Kim added, “It’s been the most special calling being your mom. I love you forever and got you forever, my bubs.”

As we've seen time and time again, North definitely inherited her mother's love of fashion and great accessories. So it seems fitting that Kim's birthday post to her mini-me would include a close-up of personalized rings that spell out “North West."

It appears as though the two have particularly bonded through custom jewelry.

For Kim’s 44th birthday back in October, her eldest child gifted her a lavish present that surely put all the others to shame.

Sharing a video to her Instagram Story, she revealed that North had gifted her a diamond pavé necklace featuring a silver plaque with an engraving that featured an extremely Gen Alpha saying.

“Okay, North got me this diamond necklace that says, ‘Skibidi toilet.’ Wow,” Kim said in the video. As for the back of the plaque, it read “Love North 10/21/24.”
