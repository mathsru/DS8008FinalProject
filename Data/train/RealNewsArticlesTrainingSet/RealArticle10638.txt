BTS Rules Social Media With 'Fake Love' Performance at 2018 Billboard Music Awards BTS put on quite the show at the 2018 Billboard Music Awards, and everyone’s talking about it.

The K-pop boy band hit the BBMAs stage for the hugely-anticipated debut performance of their new single, “Fake Love,” just moments after collecting the top social artist award. Naturally, their fans – the ARMY – took to social media to share their thoughts on the spectacle. In short, minds were blown everywhere.

It’s been a massive weekend for BTS who, on Friday, released their new album Love Yourself. Tear and the music video for its lead track, “Fake Love.” BTS was the top-trender on Twitter in the United States as millions of fans tuned in and let fly with their good vibes during and immediately after the BBMAs ceremony.
