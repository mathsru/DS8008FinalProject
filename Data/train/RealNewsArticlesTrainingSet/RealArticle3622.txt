BUT, Shawn has his reasons.

Shawn spoke with Variety recently to talk about his HUGE transformation from a Vine star to a pop-music sensation and also touched on his elusive love life, and why he’s not in a relationship right now.

“I’m not currently dating anyone, but it’s not because I don’t have time — I don’t know if I’d be dating anyone if I was home in Pickering, either,” he explains. “It hasn’t stumbled across me, and I’m not chasing it. Of course, seeing all those other artists and people in relationships, you think, ‘Maybe it would be nice; who would be great for me?’ And that’s when you realize: ‘This is wrong. Let it be. I’m not supposed to be with anyone right now.’”
