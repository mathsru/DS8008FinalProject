Lauren Paul struggling with infection from breastfeeding After welcoming daughter Story Annabelle with husband Aaron Paul in early February, Lauren Paul revealed she is suffering from mastitis, an infection of the breast tissue.

“Recovering from labor is no joke. I’m definitely on the mend in that department but…breastfeeding,” Lauren explained on Instagram Wednesday. “While it’s been one of the most beautiful experiences of my life, I have developed Mastitis twice within 3 weeks. For those of you who don’t know what that is, it’s an infection from breastfeeding that creates severe flu-like symptoms.”

Fighting a near 104-degree fever earlier this week, Lauren, 31, said it was the “sickest” she has ever felt.

“The pain and aches were unbelievable. For the [mamas who have been there, I know you feel me,” she penned.

Lauren hopes her story will inspire and educate fellow moms and parents-to-be.

“I’m feeling a responsibility to share this so that any new [mama-to-be] reading this is aware of these potential issues. If I could give an upcoming mother any advice for this chapter it would be to become well educated about breastfeeding. Take a class, do your research or have conversations with other mothers,” Lauren offered. “There are so many things that can happen … an oversupply of milk leading to infection, your baby having trouble latching, having an undersupply… being aware of this will be so helpful so that you will know how to avoid or handle these things.”

She continued, “I wanna give a big virtual hug to all the [mamas] out there who have had any type of issue breastfeeding or are dealing with anything physically or emotionally difficult, be it with you or your child. You are not alone.”

Adjusting to life at home with a newborn, Lauren, who married Aaron, 38, in 2013, continues to gush about her growing family.

“I have never loved you more than I do right now. We are so lucky to be your girls forever,” she recently shared on social media.
