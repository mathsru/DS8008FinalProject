That Twitter-Inspired Rihanna-Lupita Heist Movie Is Actually Happening Take a deep breath. If you need an inhaler, grab that. If you don’t, grab one anyway—because this is the most exciting thing you’re going to read all day. It turns out the movie Twitter dreamt up about Rihanna and Lupita Nyong’o playing a pair of genius scammers is actually coming to life.

Entertainment Weekly reported Monday that Netflix has secured a deal for the concept at the Cannes Film Festival after a “dramatic negotiation session.” Rihanna and Nyong’o will star, Ava DuVernay will reportedly direct, and Issa Rae will write the script. Rae’s team confirmed to Vanity Fair that the project is in the works, adding that the original Twitter users who imagined the concept for this film will be credited and included in some form.

DuVernay also seemed to confirm the news on Twitter, tweeting, “We deserve nice things,” and sharing a link to the movie news on Twitter Moments.
