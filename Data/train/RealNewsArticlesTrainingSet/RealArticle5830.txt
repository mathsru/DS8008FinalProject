David and Victoria Beckham barely speak at fashion do as he schmoozes Rita Ora THE Beckhams sat with their backs to each other as their awkward cold war continued.

Jack joked about the football icon stealing the spotlight at the Royal Wedding in May.

Victoria missed out on British Womenswear Designer of the Year award at Monday’s bash.
