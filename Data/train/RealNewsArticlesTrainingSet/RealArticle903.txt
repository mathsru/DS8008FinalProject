Exes Chris Evans and Jenny Slate reunite on red carpet Chris Evans and ex-girlfriend Jenny Slate reunited Tuesday at the Los Angeles premiere of their new film, “Gifted.”

The friendly pair, who split in February after less than a year of dating, posed next to one another on the red carpet.

Evans and Slate, both 35, have addressed their past romance in recent weeks, with the “Captain America” star dubbing Slate his “favorite human.”

“She’s the best,” Evans told People. “I’ve never ever ever met someone in my life who has a mastery of the English language the way she does. She’ll give you one sentence and there’s no fat to it. You’re like, ‘You just chose such an unbelievable collection of words that beautifully articulate what you say.'”

As for Slate, she has no regrets about their past.

“We’re not on bad terms, but we haven’t really seen each other, spoken a lot. I think it’s probably best,” she shared with Vulture. “I’d love to be his friend one day, but we threw down pretty hard. No regrets, though. Ever.”
