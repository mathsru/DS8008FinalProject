Atlanta rapper Gucci Mane and his fiancée Keyshia Ka’Oir are set to walk down the aisle on October 17th. On the eve of their wedding day, the couple couldn’t resist showering each other with love…and extravagant gifts.

During their rehearsal dinner, the couple wore matching red formal attire and were all smiles as they swapped early wedding presents.

Before officially becoming Mrs. Davis, Ka’Oir gifted her groom-to-be with a necklace that says “Mr. Davis” and a diamond-encrusted bow tie chain that she requested he wear during their nuptials.

But the gifts didn’t stop there. Gucci had a surprise in store for his bride-to-be that left her week in the knees.

The star bought Ka’Oir a turquoise Rolls Royce Wraith, and of course he got one too!

Gucci, born Radric Davis, proposed to the Jamaican beauty in November of 2016 with a 25-carat ring while they sat courtside at an Atlanta Hawks basketball game.

The Wopsters will officially become one in a live wedding special airing on BET which will be followed by a 10-episode docuseries chronicling the events leading up to the wedding, including their bachelor and bachelorette parties.

Congrats to the soon-to-be newlyweds!
