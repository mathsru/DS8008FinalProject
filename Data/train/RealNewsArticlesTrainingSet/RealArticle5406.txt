Marvel Studios' Black Widow - Official Teaser Trailer

Despite multiple delays, it seems as though Black Widow is finally on its way.

‌



Scarlett Johansson's Natasha Romanoff is the most prolific female heroine in the Marvel Cinematic Universe and has appeared in a number of its films.

‌



First appearing in a small role in Iron Man 2, the character achieved great popularity following her major turns in Avengers Assemble and then Captain America: The Winter Soldier.

‌



In recent years, Black Widow has continued to play a major role in the Avengers line-up, appearing in Avengers: Age of Ultron, Captain America: Civil War, Avengers: Infinity War, and also in Avengers: Endgame, where the character tragically lost her life.

It had been long hoped for, however, that the MCU would produce a solo entry for the flexible Russian spy, and now it's almost here.

With the film now finally on its way (after multiple delays), The Mirror breaks down all we know about the Widow spinning her own web...

‌

Black Widow cast

Scarlett Johansson as Black Widow/Natasha Romanoff

ScarJo is back to play the founding female Avenger, who was killed off in Avengers Endgame.

Florence Pugh as Yelena Belova

‌



View 7 Images Introducing Nat's 'little sister' Yelena (Image: Marvel Studios)

The British rising star has been cast as the second female lead, described as Natasha's "moral opposite".

She will portray Yelena Belova, a fellow Black Widow trained in the Red Room like Natasha.

‌



In the trailer the pair fight but then aid each other, having a sisterly bond.

David Harbour as Red Guardian/Alexei Shostakov

View 7 Images He's the Russian answer to Captain America (Image: Marvel Studios)

‌



The Stranger Things star will play the role of Russian super-soldier, Alexei.

He will apparently go on a big journey throughout the film as the character has a complex relationship with Natasha.

He is seen in the trailer reuniting with Natasha, Yelena, and Melina.

‌



Rachel Weisz as Melina Vostokoff

View 7 Images Can Nat trust Melina? (Image: Marvel Studios)

The Oscar-winning star of The Favourite will play the role of a seasoned spy trained from the Red Room who is deeply involved in a scientific experiment.

‌



Weisz has revealed that Melina is also a Black Widow.

O-T Fagbenle as Rick Mason

The Handmaid's Tale star will portray a figure from Natasha's past at SHIELD who she has a romantic history with.

‌



As with most spies, we are still suspicious about this character.

Ray Winstone as Dreykov

The Sexy Beast star has been cast in the Black Widow film, according to Variety.

‌



It has now been confirmed that he will portray the commander of the Red Room where the Black Widows are trained.

The character was referenced in Black Widow's first scene in the first Avengers film.

Black Widow plot

The film takes place following the events of Captain America: Civil War and before Avengers: Infinity War and sees Natasha's past catch up with her as a deadly experiment threatens the world.
