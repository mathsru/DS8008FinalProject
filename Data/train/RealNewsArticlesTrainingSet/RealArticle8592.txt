Scott Disick is still “insanely jealous” of Kourtney Kardashian’s boyfriend Younes Bendjima Can you believe that Scott Disick and Kourtney Kardashian split up nearly three years ago?

Kourtney, 38, has moved on with 24-year-old model Younes Bendjima, while Scott, 34, is now dating 19-year-old Sofia Richie.

But despite being “happy” in his new relationship, Scott is still jealous of Younes, who has been with Kourtney for over a year after meeting in Paris around the time of Kim’s robbery.

“Scott is still insanely jealous of Younes,” a source told E! “He just can't come to terms with the fact that Kourtney has found true happiness with someone else.

“Scott just never thought it would last this long with Younes or become so serious. Nobody really did.”

The source added that he “can't deal with someone else having Kourtney. He can't control his emotions about it and lets it all out.

“Kourtney never knows when he is going to fly off the handle or go nuts about something.”

But sadly for Scott, Kourtney and Younes’ relationship appears to be going from strength to strength. A source previously told People magazine: “It's definitely serious. No one thought their relationship would last this long, but Kourtney is very happy.

“Younes has met her kids. Kourtney’s family really likes him. He is the opposite of Scott. He doesn’t party, isn’t flashy and really treats Kourtney with a lot of respect. It’s obvious that he really cares about Kourtney.”

Kourtney and Scott were together for nine years and have three children together: sons Mason, eight, Reign, three, and daughter Penelope, five.
