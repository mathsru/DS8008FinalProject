The recently engaged couple introduced their rescue dog, Frankie.B, on Instagram.

“Meet Frankie B. our sweet little rescue pup who has stolen our hearts! Thanks @wagsandwalks for helping this little girl find her forever home,” VanCamp, 31, captioned the black-and-white photo. “We couldn’t be happier ❤️❤️❤️”.

VanCamp and Bowman, 29, began dating in 2011 and announced their engagement in May this year, but are mainly very private.
