Laguna Beach's Alex Murrel is pregnant with baby No. 2 A former Laguna Beach star is about to become a mother-of-two.

Alex Murrel revealed she is pregnant with her second child via Instagram. On Sunday, the MTV alum shared photos that showed her husband, Kyle Mark Johnson, and 1-year-old son, Levi, with the caption: 'Baby brother on his way this July #JulyBabies.'

The Laguna Beach star, better known as Alex M. on the hit show, showed off her growing baby bump in a black For Love & Lemons maxi dress with brown and white flowers.

Murrel, who has taken on her husband's last name of Johnson, chose fellow Laguna Beach star Taylor Cole to snap the photos for her big reveal. The two were best friends when they appeared on the show.

In the family shots, the brunette cradled her baby bump and posed alongside her son. The soon-to-be big brother wore a navy blue sweater, jeans, and sneakers for the special announcement.

In the comments, she revealed that her due date is July 27th.

And baby Johnson isn't the only upcoming addition to the Laguna Beach family.

Dieter Schmitz also revealed that he and his wife Isabell are expecting their first bundle of joy.
