Millie Bobby Brown Stuns in Cheerleader-Inspired Look While Sitting Front Row at Calvin Klein’s Fall 2018 Show Millie Bobby Brown was sitting front row at Tuesday night’s Calvin Klein show in New York City, but had you only taken a quick glance, you might not have recognized her. Wearing a cheerleader-inspired look from the brand, the brunette teen looked more grown up than ever.

We’ve always loved Millie for her whimsical, yet sophisticated, eye for style and it’s never been as apparent as it was last night. The actress chose a purple-skirted jumper with a large varsity “W” on her torso. Underneath, she layered a slim white turtleneck and threw a matching white leather jacket over her shoulders. Owner of the most epic collection of Audrey Hepburn-inspired flats, she chose a pair of bright white pointed toe skimmers for the event.

The collection itself was Americana-inspired, though futuristic in feel. And each model marched down a runway totally covered in popcorn to drive the point home. Expanding on his quilted patterns and overly buttoned-up and structured silhouettes from seasons past, creative director Raf Simons mixed in hints of metallics, easy silks, and sheer ruffles.

Getty Images Kelly Taub/BFA/REX/Shutterstock

Millie Bobby Brown’s velvet look was rife with youthful inspiration, yet tailored and classic at the same time. Though the star has been fairly absent from the Fashion Week circuit, she definitely came out of the cannon with a bang for this one. And, as the face of Raf Simons’ first-ever made-to-order line, it seems fitting that her first appearance of the week was at the creative director’s best show to date for the brand.
