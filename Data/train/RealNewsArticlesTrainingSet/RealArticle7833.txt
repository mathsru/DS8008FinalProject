Jessie James Decker Welcomes Baby #3 — and His Name Is Adorable And baby makes five! Jessie James Decker and husband Eric Decker welcomed their third child over the weekend, making the announcement in a pair of Instagram posts that also revealed the new bundle’s adorable name.

“Forrest Bradley Decker, born March 31st, weighing 9lbs,” the country singer captioned her first snap of their baby son, which shows him lying on her chest with a tiny knitted newborn hat on his head. “We are so in love.”

Her NFL player husband also shared a glimpse of their newest addition, this time showing off little Forrest’s full head of hair. “Welcome to this world Forrest Bradley Decker!” he wrote. “My heart is full! Wolfpack completed with a 9lb, lean, eating machine (and a Decker bucket head)! Mommy sure knows how to grow some babies!!”

The Deckers first revealed they were expecting back in October, and shortly after, got the whole family in on a video to reveal that they’d be having another boy. New baby Decker boy joins big sister Vivianne Rose, 4, and brother Eric Jr., 2.

Last month, Eric shared a still from the iconic film Forrest Gump, which fans are now realizing they should have taken as a naming sign. In the last Instagram update from Jessie before giving birth, she said that the third would be their last. Congrats to the Decker family!
