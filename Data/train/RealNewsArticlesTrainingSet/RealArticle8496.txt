Season 7 Episodes (TV Series) Jennifer Williams Cast Member

Jennifer Williams is an entrepreneur, business woman, influencer and original cast member of VH1’s “Basketball Wives.” Now in its 12th season, “Basketball Wives” is one of the first reality shows on television and one of Vh1’s highest rated series, with Williams at the forefront of driving ratings since the show’s premiere in 2010. Williams love for the camera, acting and storytelling, has inspired her latest venture of launching her own production company, Jennifer Williams Productions. The first project released on Jennifer Williams Production roster was a true story and passion project for Williams based on a heart wrenching personal experience with a serial con artist titled, “MyTrue Scam Story.” This two part series premiered on VH1 on November 6th and 13th 2023 with rave reviews and ratings. In 2025, JWP will be releasing its first motion picture, Shattered where Williams has a starring role. In 2021, Williams combined her entrepreneurial spirit with her passion for fashion and beauty together, founding Redefined Glam, a luxury hair extension brand faceted with the best hair wigs and bundles, along with hair styling products and cosmetics. Products are available via the RedefinedGlam.com website Williams attended the University of Maryland and Fairleigh Dickinson University. She graduated cum laude with a Bachelor’s Degree in Political Science. Following graduation, Williams was married to NBA star, Eric Williams, for 6 years and became a basketball wife. Williams was raised in in South Orange, NJ an upscale suburb just outside of New York City and resides in Atlanta, GA since 2020.
