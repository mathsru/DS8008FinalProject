The Platinum Life — which is in its first season — follows seven ladies who are deeply invested in LA’s music scene, thanks to their husbands and boyfriends. The show stars Alycia Bella (Ty Dolla $ign’s ex of more than 10 years), La’Myia Good (Eric Bellinger’s wife), Asiah Collins (Kid Ink’s wife), Shantel Jackson (Nelly’s girlfriend), Crystal Smith (Ne-Yo’s wife), Lola Monroe (King Los’ partner), and Nazanin Mandi (Miguel’s fiancée).

While the ladies have it great in some ways — they get to live the luxurious life, with access to vacations and parties most of us can only dream of — there are some down sides to being with a musician: namely, all the time they spend on tour. Fortunately, the ladies are showing the ups and downs on the show — and we know we’ll be watching every second of it!
