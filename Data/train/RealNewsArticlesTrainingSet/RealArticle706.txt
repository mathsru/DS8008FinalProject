Princess Eugenie and Jack Brooksbank Are Inviting Members of the Public To Their Wedding Princess Eugenie has arrived at King Charles III's coronation.



The royal was photographed arriving at Westminster Abbey in London this morning alongside her father, Prince Andrew, and her husband Jack Brooksbank.

Niall Carson - PA Images // Getty Images

WPA Pool // Getty Images

The couple walked in with Princess Beatrice, her husband, Edoardo Mapelli Mozzi, and Prince Harry, and the five took a seat together to watch King Charles and Camilla be crowned.

WPA Pool // Getty Images

For the grand occasion, Eugenie wore an elegant navy dress with a matching round hat, long black coat, and silky navy sandals. She accessorized with a large diamond collar necklace and diamond drop earrings by Garrard, and a black handbag. Her hair was styled in a sophisticated wavy 'do, and she wore subtle dark eye makeup.

Jack looked dapper in a black suit, white shirt, and light blue tie to match his wife's ensemble.

Harry and Edoardo also kept things classic in their elegant black suits. Beatrice's hot pink outfit, meanwhile, contrasted her sister's.

Eugenie and Jack share son August Philip Hawke Brooksbank and have another baby on the way. Their two-year-old was not present at the coronation.

The coronation of King Charles is taking place today, Saturday, May 6. Camilla, Queen Consort, will also be coronated alongside her husband. Following the main ceremony, there will be two more days of coronation festivities: a Coronation Big Lunch and Coronation Concert on Sunday, May 7, and a nationwide volunteering event called The Big Help Out on Monday, May 8.

Eugenie, sister Beatrice, and other members of the royal family—including Prince William and Princess Catherine—are set to attend.
