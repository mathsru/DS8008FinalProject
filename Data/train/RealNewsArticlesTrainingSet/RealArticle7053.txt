All Chicago West Baby Photos Timeline Kim Kardashian just shared a series of adorable photos with her youngest daughter, Chicago West, proving that the five-year-old has already mastered the art of the selfie. (Like mother, like daughter.)

In the close-ups shared on Instagram today, Kardashian is seen posing alongside her mini-me, who wears a black top just like her. In the first photo, the Kardashians star is seen giving Chicago a kiss on the cheek. In the second, Chi flashes a peace sign, and both her and her famous mom show off their cutest duck face. They make the same lip pucker in the third photo, where they look even more like twins.

Kardashian captioned the sweet mother-daughter carousel with a simple pink heart emoji.

Kardashian shares Chicago, as well as kids North, 9, Saint, 7, and Psalm, 3, with ex-husband Kanye West. The SKIMS founder regularly posts photos of her children on social media.

Earlier this month, she shared a snap of Chi with cousin True Thompson, both dressed to the nines in coordinating looks including sunglasses, mini purses, and flashy cowboy boots. "When you ask why they're late for dinner… 😂💕," Kardashian wrote in the caption.

And last month, Kardashian shared a photo of Chicago with cousin Dream Kardashian; in it, both wear pink pajamas and matching pink hair wraps. "Baby love 💕," Kardashian captioned the post.

Meanwhile, Kardashian's eldest daughter, North West, holds the creative reins over the reality star's TikTok. The two have a joint account, @kimandnorth, where they frequently post humorous videos of them participating in viral trends and collaborating with stars including Ice Spice and Mariah Carey.

"I found a groove with [North's] TikTok," Kardashian previously said of her daughter's TikTok presence to Gwyneth Paltrow on an episode of The Goop Podcast. "As long as it's me and her together and I'm there, that works for now. But it's a crazy world, I don't deny that."
