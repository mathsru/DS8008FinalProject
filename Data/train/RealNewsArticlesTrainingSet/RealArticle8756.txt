All the celebrity couples that broke up in 2018 For years, Kendra Wilkinson and Hank Baskett spoke openly about their marital problems, and their relationship seemed to take a toll when news spread in 2014 of Baskett's reported cheating while Wilkinson was pregnant with their second child.

"I messed up," Baskett told People during an interview. "I put myself in a bad situation. And everything through me brought pain upon this family."

Prior to their split, there were reports stating that the couple was planning on separating after being married since 2009. Wilkinson seemingly confirmed it on Instagram on Monday, April 2.

Taking to her Instagram story, Wilkinson shared several photos and emotional videos addressing her relationship with Baskett.

"Ten years," she said. "I did everything I could. It wasn't good enough. I will always love him and my heart will always remain open for him. I believed in forever, I really did. Guess it's just not meant to be. I'm so scared, but I have to get strong for my kids — I will."

She added: "I never thought I'd see the day, really."

Wilkinson also shared a photo from their wedding day with the words "I believed."

On her Twitter, she gave further insight into what she's been experiencing and how she plans to move forward.

She wrote: "Today will be the saddest, scariest day of my life. Today i will have to be the strongest I've ever been. Today, my rebirth begins."
