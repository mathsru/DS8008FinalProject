Khloé Kardashian Reveals Her Sweet Nickname For Daughter True Despite her relative social media silence in recent weeks, since the arrival of her daughter True (and boyfriend Tristan Thompson’s cheating scandal), Khloé Kardashian is slowly teasing out details about her little one to her fans.

Yesterday, following boyfriend Tristan’s opening up about his daughter, the 33-year-old new mum took to Twitter to reveal the adorable nickname she has for her one-month-old.

‘I can’t believe my baby will be a month old tomorrow,’ the reality star wrote on Twitter Wednesday.

‘Why does this make me happy and sad all at the same time,’ admitted the new mother before revealing her baby girl’s nickname.

‘Thank you Jesus for my sweet peanut,’ she gushed.

Minutes later, the fashion mogul thanked her fans for their continued love and support shown to her ‘sweet peanut’. ‘I love you thank you for the sweet love to baby True. Night night babes,’ she added.

Earlier in the day the star, who welcomed daughter True with Thompson on 12 April, shared her plans for her first Mother’s Day this Sunday on her app and website.

‘I’ve always loved Mother’s Day and celebrating Kris Jenner, MJ and my sisters — but this year will be even more special because now I’m a mommy, too!’ Khloé wrote referring to her mother and grandmother Mary Jo.

‘As of now, there aren’t any special plans (that I know of, LOL). I’m really enjoying spending quality time with True and loving just hibernating with her,’ she concluded her post.

On the same day, Thompson spoke publicly about the couple’s daughter for the first time during a Road Trippin podcast.

The 27-year-old told the hosts that True was ‘doing good’. ‘Baby True is eating, sleeping and, uh, sh*tting,’ he shared. ‘That’s all they do.’
