Dancing with the Stars (U.S. season 25) Season of television series

Season of television series

Season twenty-five of Dancing with the Stars premiered on September 18, 2017, on the ABC network.

On November 21, 2017, actor and singer Jordan Fisher and Lindsay Arnold were crowned the champions, while violinist Lindsey Stirling and Mark Ballas finished in second place, and actor Frankie Muniz and Witney Carson finished in third.

Cast [ edit ]

Couples [ edit ]

This season featured thirteen celebrity contestants. Good Morning America announced the professional dancers who would be competing in the upcoming season on August 27, 2017.[2] Alan Bersten, a member of the troupe since season 20, was promoted to pro. Drew Scott was announced as the first celebrity competing for the season, partnered with Emma Slater.[3] On September 6, the rest of the cast was revealed on Good Morning America.[4]

Hosts and judges [ edit ]

Tom Bergeron and Erin Andrews returned as hosts, while Carrie Ann Inaba, Len Goodman, and Bruno Tonioli returned as judges.[15] Julianne Hough did not return as a permanent judge this season, though she did return as a guest judge during the first night of the finals.[16] On October 23, singer Shania Twain joined the panel as a guest judge.[17]

Dance troupe [ edit ]

The troupe consisted of former pros Sasha Farber and Jenna Johnson, and prior troupe members Artur Adamski, Brandon Armstrong, Hayley Erbert, and Britt Stewart.[18]

Week 3: Guilty Pleasures Night [ edit ]

The couples performed one unlearned dance to a song that reminded them of their secret guilty pleasure. Couples are listed in the order they performed.

At the start of the show, a moment of silence was held to honor the victims of the 2017 Las Vegas shooting, which had occurred the previous day.[23] It was announced at the beginning of the show that, due to the tragedy, no elimination would take place.

Vanessa Lachey performed with Alan Bersten this week while Maksim Chmerkovskiy was absent dealing with a "personal matter".[24]

Week 4: Most Memorable Year Night [ edit ]

The couples danced one unlearned dance to celebrate the most memorable year of their lives. Couples are listed in the order they performed.

Week 5: Disney Night [ edit ]

The couples performed one unlearned dance to a song from a Disney film. All performances were chronological based on when the films were released.

Week 7: Halloween Night [ edit ]

The couples performed one unlearned dance and a team dance. Couples are listed in the order they performed. Two couples were eliminated at the end of the night.

Week 8: Quarterfinals [ edit ]

Week 9: Semifinals [ edit ]

The couples performed an unlearned dance using music chosen by the pros, as well as a dance that reinterpreted an iconic routine from a previous season. Couples are listed in the order they performed.
