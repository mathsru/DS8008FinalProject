Carrie Underwood Posts Photo of Her Face 5 Months After Stitches Carrie Underwood is back in the music studio — and on social media — after injuring herself in a fall, and she's providing a rare glimpse of herself to prove it.The singer took to Twitter and Instagram to share a black and white photo of herself facing sideways, hard at work on new music, marking one of the few times the world has seen her face this year. The singer fell down the steps of her Nashville home in November, which resulted in a broken wrist and a facial injury that required 40 stitches. Since the fall, Underwood has largely stayed off of social media, and in the last six months, has only shared a few carefully composed shots of her face — seemingly not yet ready to reveal herself post-injury.

In January, Underwood opened up about how she was feeling in the wake of the fall and her medical treatment. "There is also another part of the story that I haven't been ready to talk about since I have still been living it and there has been much uncertainty as to how things will end up. It's crazy how a freak random accident can change your life," she wrote in a letter to her fan club, People reported. "In addition to breaking my wrist, I somehow managed to injure my face as well. I’ll spare you the gruesome details, but when I came out of surgery the night of my fall, the doctor told [Underwood’s husband] Mike [Fisher] that he had put between 40 and 50 stitches in." She later shared that she was recovering well, but "not quite looking the same."
