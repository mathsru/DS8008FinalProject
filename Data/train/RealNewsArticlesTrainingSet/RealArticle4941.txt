SEE IT: Nicki Minaj cozies up to Nas in flirty Instagram photo sparking dating rumors Nicki Minaj is still keeping Nas right by her side.

The rappers played each other’s love interest in Minaj’s steamy 2012 music video for “Right By My Side,” but it appears things are starting to heat up for them in the real world.

The “Anaconda” rapper sparked rumors she’s dating fellow hip-hop artist Nas after she posted a cozy Instagram photo of them snuggling up together at Los Angeles’ Sweet Chick restaurant.

In the picture, the “If I Ruled The World” rapper is seen with his arm draped around her shoulder as he leans in towards the rapper. Minaj has her eyes closed as she rests against his chest.

“Only KINGS recognize QUEENS,” she wrote in the caption. “@sweetchicklife has the best food. congrats @nas.”

Nas re-posted the image on his account, adding the words: “Straight Outta Queens!”

It appears the pair was celebrating the recent opening of the chicken and waffle eatery, which Nas helped expand to the West Coast. There are already three Sweet Chick restaurants in New York.

Sources told Us Weekly that Minaj, 34, and Nas, 43, were “laughing a lot” while they dined at the restaurant.

“They looked very cute together,” the insider said. “No PDA, but they looked like a couple.”

Minaj split from Meek Mill last year. Before that she dated rapper and “Love & Hip Hop: Hollywood” star Safaree Samuels. Nas was previously married to singer Kelis.
