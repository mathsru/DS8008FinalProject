Kris Jenner Says Khloé Kardashian and True Thompson Will Be Home in LA Tristan Thompson Khloé Kardashian and Tristan Thompson are having another child together.

The exes, who spent years reconciling following Thompson's various cheating scandals, made the decision to give their four-year-old daughter, True Thompson, a sibling late last year, when they were on good terms and thinking about getting back together.

A representative for Kardashian told Entertainment Tonight, "We can confirm True will have a sibling who was conceived in November. Khloé is incredibly grateful to the extraordinary surrogate for such a beautiful blessing. We'd like to ask for kindness and privacy so that Khloé can focus on her family."

The news comes as viewers saw Thompson's latest cheating scandal—where he fathered a child with a personal trainer with whom he had an affair—unfold on The Kardashians. Though Thompson initially denied the rumors in December, he eventually took a paternity test and confirmed the news.

"Today, paternity test results reveal that I fathered a child with Maralee Nichols," Thompson wrote on an Instagram Story at the time. "I take full responsibility for my actions. Now that paternity has been established I look forward to amicably raising our son."

He also publicly apologized to Kardashian, saying she did not deserve how he treated her.

After learning of the paternity scandal through an online article, Kardashian said on the show, "It's a joke, it's gross. It's a joke, it's embarrassing, it's disgusting, we're all over it. I'm like, 'I didn't buy tickets to this fucking circus, but somehow I'm watching all these clowns act out in front of me.'"

Still, a source told ET the Good American cofounder is happy to become a mom again and "wants Tristan in both the kids' lives as much as he wants to be."
