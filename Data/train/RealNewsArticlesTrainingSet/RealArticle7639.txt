All the winners and speeches of the GQ Men of the Year Awards 2017 Last summer, something extraordinary happened. A group of completely unknown teenagers cycled onto our Netflix accounts in one of the best TV shows of the year. Within weeks, they were no longer kids, but world-famous actors, and we were talking about The Upside Down and asking, “Where’s Barb?” Within months, those kids were award-winning too.The well deserved winners of the GQ Editor’s Special Award are the boys of Stranger Things, Caleb McLaughlin, Finn Wolfhard, Gaten Matarazzo and Noah Schnapp!

Creative Maverick of the Year: Sergei Polunin

Presented by Erin O'Connor

Doug Inglish

This winner has never done things by the book. One of the most gifted ballerinas of his generation, he quit the Royal Ballet just two years after becoming the youngest principal dancer in its history, going on not only to star in a David LaChapelle video set to Hozier’s “Take Me To The Church”, which has been viewed more than 20 million times, but parlayed that into a film career that will see him star in Kenneth Brannagh’s Murder On The Orient Express later this year. He’s known as the “bad boy of ballet”, but he looks pretty good to us.
