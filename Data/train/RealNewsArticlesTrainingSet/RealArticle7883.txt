Critics' Choice Awards 2018: The Complete List of Nominations Awards season is heating up!

The 2018 Critics' Choice Awards nominations were revealed on Wednesday and ET was with This Is Us star Chrissy Metz right after she landed a nomination for Best Supporting Actress in a Drama Series.

"It's really cool. Movin' on up! I found out as [I was] driving here and it's so wonderful," she told ET's Keltie Knight at The Hollywood Reporter's 2017 Women in Entertainment Breakfast at Los Angeles' Milk Studios.

Chrissy Metz attends The Hollywood Reporter's 2017 Women In Entertainment Breakfast at Milk Studios on Dec. 6 in Los Angeles, California. - Getty Images

In addition to Metz's nomination, her co-star, Sterling K. Brown, is up for Best Actor in a Drama Series and the show itself has been nominated for Best Drama Series.

The This Is Us family isn't alone in celebrating their Critics' Choice Awards nods. Guillermo del Toro's movie, The Shape of Water -- which stars Sally Hawkins, Michael Shannon, Richard Jenkins and Octavia Spencer -- leads with a whopping 14 nominations, including Best Picture, Best Actress, Best Supporting Actor, Best Supporting Actor and Best Director.

Get Out, The Big Sick, Girls Trip and I, Tonya also got a few nominations as well as Game of Thrones, Black-ish, GLOW and Billions.
