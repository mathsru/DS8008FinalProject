Reese Witherspoon, lookalike daughter Ava Phillippe twin at 'Home Again' premiere Aug. 30, 2017, 10:02 a.m. ET

The strong likeness of Reese Witherspoon and Ava Phillippe was front and center at Tuesday's Home Again premiere.

The mother-daughter duo who (almost eerily) seem to share the same face attended the L.A. event for the movie starring Witherspoon, Nat Wolff and Lake Bell out next week.

They didn't coordinate their ensembles to the level they did for February's Big Little Lies premiere. But they don't have to for the resemblance to show.

Tuesday, the Wrinkle in Time actress, 41, wore a cherry, knee-length Roland Mouret dress. Her 17-year-old daughter with Ryan Phillippe chose a black and cream animal-print design with transparent, 3/4 sleeves. However, their poses were perfectly in sync.
