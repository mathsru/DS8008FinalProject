Golden Globes 2018: Lady Bird Wins Best Motion Picture, Musical or Comedy Lady Bird has won Best Motion Picture, Musical or Comedy at the 2018 Golden Globes. It was up against James Franco’s The Disaster Artist, Jordan Peele’s Get Out, The Greatest Showman, and I, Tonya. Lady Bird, the coming of age tale starring Saoirse Ronan, was written and directed by Greta Gerwig. Ronan won for Best Actress in a Musical or Comedy Motion Picture, Laurie Metcalf was up for Best Supporting Actress in a Motion Picture, and Gerwig was nominated for Best Screenplay. Jon Brion scored Lady Bird’s soundtrack, which is out on vinyl on February 23.
