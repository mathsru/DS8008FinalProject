Kim Kardashian Just Shaded Lamar Odom with a Tweet About Brothels and We've Never Seen Her So Savage

When you mess with one member of the Kardashian family, you mess with them all. It looks like Lamar Odom has learned that the hard way after making some recent shady comments about ex-wife Khloe Kardashian.

Lamar expressed his well wishes for Khloe as she prepares to welcome her first child into the world during an appearance on the new series Mancave, which premiered on BET this week. However, the retired NBA player also seemed to take some jabs at his ex. “I still got my shawty’s name still on me, her initials still on me,” the former Los Angeles Lakers star said on Mancave via Us Weekly. “But for no reason. You know what I’m saying? I understand when it’s over, it’s over. When she was with her second or third NBA ball player, I could see that.”





After the couple separated in 2013, Khloe would go on to date the Houston Rockets' James Harden, and she is currently expecting her first child with the Cleveland Cavaliers' Tristan Thompson. Khloe and Lamar finalized their divorce in 2016.

Well, Khloe's older sister Kim Kardashian was having none of Lamar's comments. Kim responded to a tweet featuring Lamar's shade with a scorching burn of her own, writing "or second or third brothel." What Kim is referencing here is when Lamar grabbed headlines in 2015 after he was hospitalized after being found unconscious in a Nevada brothel.
