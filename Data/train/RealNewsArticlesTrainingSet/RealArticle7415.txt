Saturdays are family days for Jennifer Lopez!

The 48-year-old singer took her twins, Max and Emme, and Alex Rodriguez's two daughters, Natasha and Ella, to the Mets vs. Dodgers game at Citi Field in Queens, New York, on Saturday, while the former baseball player was covering the game for Fox Sports.

"So, here we are at Citi Field. We're having a family day with all the kiddies and grandma," Lopez said in her Instagram story.

The couple regularly share their family moments on social media. Most recently, the Shades of Blue star posted an adorable pic of Rodriguez cuddling with his kids.
