John Mayer Tried to Shoot his Shot With Nicki Minaj If you follow John Mayer on Twitter, then Friday night seemed to offer some form of proof that Mayer has reversed course on his 2010 comments about a certain member of his anatomy agreeing with white supremacist David Duke regarding interracial dating. The object of his Twitter affection quickly replied with a pun based on one of Mayer’s more popular hits, replying, “Would my body be your wonderland?”

And then two fan bases queued up their “pair of eyeballs” emojis to weigh in on the matter. Such activity would usually provide an opportunity for some hyperbole about the internet collectively “losing it,” but Mayer already took care of that for everyone by further replying, “Please hold, losing my shit. This isn’t my reply yet.”
