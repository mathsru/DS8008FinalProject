Inside Eva Longoria’s star-studded, PJ-themed baby shower Eva Longoria celebrated the imminent birth of her first child with a pajama-party-themed shower with her pals, including Elizabeth Banks, Melanie Griffith, Sophia Bush and Roselyn Sanchez at the Lombardi House in LA.

The starry crew were treated to massages, hair and makeup, and manicures.

Guests picked newborn gifts from Longoria’s Amazon baby registry.

The “Desperate Housewives” star is expecting a son with husband, Jose Antonio Bastón.
