Khloé Kardashian Throws 'Cousin Cupcake Party' for True Thompson's Half-Birthday Khloé Kardashian celebrated ex Tristan Thompson's birthday by writing a lengthy statement praising him and sharing photos of their little family.

In her Instagram post, Kardashian included intimate photos of Thompson and their kids: daughter True Thompson, 4, and their baby son. It marks the first time we've gotten a real glimpse of the seven-month-old, whose name his parents have yet to publicly reveal.

"Happy birthday @realtristan13 You are truly the best father, brother & uncle. Your love, attention, silly dances, hugs, carpool rides, bed time rituals, the way u show up for them. All of the above means more than you'll ever know to your family of littles," Kardashian wrote in the caption. "My birthday wish for u is that u continue to crave change, healing, and transformation. Be strong, be kind, be patient, be free. Continue to Make your soul and your mommy proud. Happy birthday baby daddy."

Thompson turned 32 on March 13.

In the first photo of the carousel, Thompson is seen gazing lovingly at his and Kardashian's baby son while embracing daughter True and his son Prince, 6, on the couch (Thompson shares Prince with ex Jordan Craig). The baby wears a black onesie with a white cartoon ghost print.

Another sweet photo shows the baby looking intently at Thompson as he leans on his chest.

Kardashian and Thompson confirmed in August 2022 that they had welcomed their second child, a son via surrogate. On her family's Hulu show, The Kardashians, the Good American cofounder opened up about her and Thompson's decision to have another child, and spoke candidly about how the experience was ruined for her by the NBA player's infamous paternity scandal.
