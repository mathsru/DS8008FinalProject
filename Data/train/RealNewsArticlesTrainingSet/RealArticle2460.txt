Listen to Harry Styles' first solo single 'Sign Of The Times' YOU GUYS, Harry Styles from One Direction just played his debut solo single 'Sign Of The Times' on BBC Radio 1 during a 2-hour interview with Nick Grimshaw - AND IT'S SO GOOD.

Since One Direction went on hiatus last summer, Harry Styles has been a busy little bee - after landing a role in Christopher Nolan's Dunkirk and going to Jamaica to record his debut solo album.

Today, he released the first single 'Sign Of The Times'. So, you wanna hear it? HERE IT IS:

Yeah, we're obsessed.

Harry also spoke to Nick for two hours (TWO HOURS) about everything from what Adele gave him for his birthday to his 'weird' and 'wrong' approach to dating.

On the topic of his forthcoming album, Harry said he was nervous about the reaction it would receive, but also incredibly proud of it. He commented:

"It’s a bit weird, I feel like I’ve been hibernating for so long now and you hear it in the safety of the studio and now it’s time to give birth … it’s the song (debut single) I’m most proud of writing.

"In the least weird way possible, it’s my favourite album to listen to at the moment… I think if you put out something that you don’t stand behind and really love…then if it doesn’t go well then you could regret not doing what you wanted to do. Whereas if nothing happens with it, I love it you know so I think that’s what you should do."

Getty Images

On what inspired the album, Harry added:

“I think a lot of people, especially at labels separate it because they work in music that it’s a broader understanding of music than people who are listening… I think we are all fans of music and I think we made it as the fan… I hope we did a good job but I really like the album so I hope people like it.”
