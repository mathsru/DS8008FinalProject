Sarah Michelle Gellar & Selma Blair Have a 'Cruel Intentions' Reunion at P!nk Concert Trending on Billboard

Sarah Michelle Gellar and Selma Blair reunited for a sweet selfie at P!nk‘s LA concert this weekend at The Forum.

Gellar posted the picture, gushing with her co-star from the 1999 film Cruel Intentions, in which the two actresses were anything but on good terms (although their characters did share an infamous onscreen kiss). She not only called Blair “the best date ever,” but described P!nk as “F**kin Perfect,” to quote her 2010 hit single.

The Buffy the Vampire Slayer star posted another Instagram of the performer with her 8-year-old daughter Charlotte Grace and her friend citing the lyrics of another P!nk pop classic: “Oh…we got the party started @pink.”

Blair posted the same selfie of the two of them with a similarly sweet caption: “We have big smiles cause we were together @pink concert and loved every minute.”
