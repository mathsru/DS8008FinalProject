Imagine heading to your local discount store to do some last minute holiday shopping and seeing...Kate Middleton, Duchess of Cambridge, filling up her cart with loads of gifts. This really happened in Norfolk, England over the weekend.

On Saturday, Kate Middleton was spotted at The Range store in Kings Lynn, England along with her children, Princess Charlotte and Prince George. Shoppers easily recognized the Duchess' children, who was holding their mother's hand, but Kate blended in with the crowd of last-minute shoppers in an olive green Dubarry Bracken tweed jacket (according to Kate Middleton fan page @middletonmaven) with her hair pulled back into a loose ponytail.

Of course, Kate's security detail were nearby but gave the mother a considerable amount of distance "so they looked like any other family out grabbing last-minute Christmas bargains," one shopper told The Sun.

One mother even tried to snap a photo with the Duchess but was turned down. "When Kate got to the checkouts I plucked up courage to ask if she’d do a selfie with me and my kids. She very politely declined," the shopper said. "She was so nice, explaining she was just doing a bit of Christmas shopping."

Ah Royals, they're just like us. Sorta.
