Lake Bell and husband Scott Campbell have welcomed their second child, a baby boy, Us Weekly confirmed.

No name or birthdate have been confirmed by the couple, who wed in 2013 after meeting on the set of "How to Make It in America" two years earlier.

This is the second child for the 38-year-old actress and her husband, a tattoo artist — they are also parents to 2-year-old daughter Nova.
