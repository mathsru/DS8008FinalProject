Victoria Beckham meeting David Beckham was just one of those inevitable things.

She was one-fifth of pop sensation the Spice Girls and he was an up-and-coming Manchester United midfielder. Together, they became Posh and Becks, the U.K.'s most glamorous couple. They quickly transcended the confines of regional fame and turned into a full-on global sensation, paparazzi snapping away wherever they went, in good times and bad.

Family feud rumors aside, times have been pretty grand lately for Sir and Lady Beckham, David having received a knighthood from King Charles III in June for his services to sport and charity.

And yet times got particularly bad for a spell, after David's former assistant Rebecca Loos said she had an affair with the footballer. He adamantly denied it, but to this day she maintains that she was telling it as it was.

David and Victoria, who are celebrating 26 years of marriage on July 4, didn't comment on her latest assertion.

But they did address that painful period of their lives in the 2023 Netflix docuseries Beckham.
