Interlocking G tights Gucci upholds recognized international standards of social and environmental responsibility across all its operations. From selecting and tracing raw materials to designing and crafting our products, we prioritize a circular approach that maintains our long-standing commitment to quality and durability.

Through ongoing innovation and optimization of our production processes, we strive to minimize the environmental footprint of our business and the entire supply chain. Our approach involves transforming our sourcing strategy by investing in new sourcing solutions, lower impact materials, and regenerative agriculture projects for the production of the raw materials used in our collections.
