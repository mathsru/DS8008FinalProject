Jennifer Lopez Tickets Jennifer Lopez is a singer, actress and dancer from the Bronx, New York. After getting her start as a Fly Girl on In Living Color and headlining films like Selena (1997) and Out of Sight (1998), Lopez began releasing music with her 1999 debut album On the 6. Across eight more albums — including J.Lo (2001), This Is Me… Then (2002), Rebirth (2005), the Spanish – language Como Ama una Mujer (2007), Brave (2007), Love? (2011) and A.K.A. (2014) — the singer has notched 10 Top 10 hits on the Billboard Hot 100, including chart – toppers “If You Had My Love,” “Ain’t It Funny” featuring Ja Rule, “All I Have” featuring LL Cool J and “I’m Real.” Lopez’s 2024 album, This Is Me… Now, serves as a sequel to her 2002 effort This Is Me… Then, and was released with the accompanying Amazon Prime Video musical film This Is Me… Now: A Love Story. In 2025-26, Lopez is taking over Vegas for Jennifer Lopez: The JLo Show Live in Las Vegas.
