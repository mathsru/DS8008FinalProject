Jennifer Aniston Friends News, Photos and Style Updates Jennifer Aniston

Actress Jennifer Aniston was born in California in 1969. She is best known for her role as Rachel Green on the NBC television sitcom Friends from 1994 to 2004, for which she won a Primetime Emmy Award and a Golden Globe. Aniston has also starred in several successful films such as Marley & Me (2008), The Break-Up (2006) and Horrible Bosses (2011). She has featured in several magazine lists of the world's most beautiful women. Aniston was married to fellow actor Brad Pitt 2000-2005 and Justin Theroux 2015-2017.
