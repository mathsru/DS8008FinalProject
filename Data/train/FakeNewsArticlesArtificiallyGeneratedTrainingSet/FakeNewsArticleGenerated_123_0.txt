Andrew Dominik, the acclaimed film director, has made headlines once again with his latest project. This time, he's not behind the camera, but rather in front of it - as a contestant on Jimmy Kimmel's popular game show, "Celebrity Antics."

    Dominik, known for his work on films like "The Assassination of Jesse James by the Coward Robert Ford" and "Chopper," has never been one to shy away from a challenge. And when he received an invitation to appear on the show, he jumped at the opportunity.

    "I'm always up for a good time, and I thought it would be a fun way to shake things up," Dominik said in an interview. "Plus, I've always been a fan of the show."

    During his appearance on the show, Dominik was paired with comedian and actress, Jen Kirkman, to compete in a series of wacky challenges. One of the most memorable moments came when the couple was tasked with dressing up in ridiculous costumes and acting out a scene from a famous movie.

    Dominik, who opted to dress up as a former gangster, got into character and began to deliver a dramatic monologue. However, things took a turn when he accidentally let slip a major spoiler from the latest episode of his favorite TV show. The audience gasped in shock, and Kirkman couldn't help but burst out laughing.

    "I was so caught up in the moment, I forgot that not everyone had seen the episode yet," Dominik admitted. "But hey, at least it made for good TV, right?"

    Despite the mishap, Dominik's friend and former collaborator, director and producer, Barry Gifford, was impressed with his performance. "Andrew's always been a bit of a wild card, and this was no exception," Gifford said. "I'm just glad he didn't get voted off the island - yet."

    In the end, Dominik and Kirkman managed to come in second place, just behind a couple who had been on the show before. While he may not have won the competition, Dominik left with a newfound appreciation for the show and a promise to return for another episode.

    "I had an amazing time, and I'm already looking forward to the next challenge," Dominik said. "Who knows, maybe I'll even get to wear a dress next time."
