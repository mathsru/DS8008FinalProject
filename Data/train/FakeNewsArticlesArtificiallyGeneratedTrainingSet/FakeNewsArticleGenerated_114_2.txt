Justin Theroux's Trivia Night Raises Funds for Charity
-----------------------------------------------

Justin Theroux's annual trivia night has become a staple in the charity circuit, and this year's event was no exception. The star-studded gathering, which took place on January 15th, saw many familiar faces come together to test their knowledge and raise funds for a good cause.

Among the attendees were friends of the actor, including Nicole Contreras and Park, who showed up in full force to support the charity. According to sources, the pair was seen laughing and having a great time, even though they didn't exactly ace the trivia competition.

One of the highlights of the night was a special appearance by country music star Tracy Byrd, who took to the stage to perform a few of his hit songs. The crowd was thoroughly entertained, and the singer's rendition of his classic hit "Put a Little Love in Your Heart" had everyone singing along.

But the real pièce de résistance was the announcement of the winner of the trivia competition. After a tense and thrilling finale, it was revealed that judge Tracy Byrd had emerged victorious, much to the surprise of the other contestants. The singer was beaming with pride as he accepted the prize, a trophy and a statement piece of jewelry donated by a local designer.

The event, which was held at a luxurious vacation rental in the Hollywood Hills, raised a significant amount of money for the charity. According to the organizers, the total amount raised was $50,000, which will go towards supporting various causes, including education and healthcare initiatives.

When asked about the event, Justin Theroux released a statement expressing his gratitude to all who attended and supported the charity. "I'm thrilled that our trivia night was such a success," he said. "It's always amazing to see so many wonderful people come together for a good cause. I'm already looking forward to next year's event!"

The charity event was a wonderful way to spend a Christmas evening, surrounded by friends and like-minded individuals who share a passion for giving back. As the holiday season comes to a close, it's heartwarming to know that there are still people out there making a difference in the world, one trivia question at a time.
