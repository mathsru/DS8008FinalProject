    According to sources close to the actress, the thieves made off with a stash of high-end jewellery worth tens of thousands of dollars. The stolen items included a diamond necklace, a pair of earrings, and a gold watch. Brosnahan was devastated by the loss, and her team immediately launched an investigation into the theft.

    In a statement released to the press, Brosnahan expressed her gratitude to the hotel staff and security team for their help in dealing with the aftermath of the theft. "I want to thank the hotel and their staff for their kindness and assistance during this difficult time," she said. "Their professionalism and compassion were greatly appreciated."

    Meanwhile, police are investigating the theft and are reviewing security footage to identify the culprits. A user on social media, who claimed to have seen the thieves, believed that they were a group of young men who had been harassing other guests at the hotel. The user helped police by providing a detailed description of the suspects, which has aided the investigation.

The app, which was launched last year, aims to provide a safe and supportive community for women who have experienced harassment or discrimination in the industry.

    Despite the setback, Brosnahan remains committed to her charity work and is using her platform to raise awareness about the importance of helping others. In a statement, she said, "I believe that everyone deserves to be treated with kindness and respect. By helping others, we can create a more compassionate and just society."
