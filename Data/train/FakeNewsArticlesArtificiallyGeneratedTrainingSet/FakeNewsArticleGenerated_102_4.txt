Kanye West Is Promoting a New Video, Shared by Stephen Hawking, Featuring Angelina Jolie and Donna Kelce During an Eclipse

Renowned physicist Stephen Hawking has joined forces with rapper Kanye West to create a unique and captivating video. The video, titled "Matrix Eclipse," features Angelina Jolie and NFL player Donna Kelce in a thrilling performance that is sure to leave audiences in awe.

According to sources close to the production, the video was filmed during the rare solar eclipse on July 2nd. West and Hawking wanted to create something that would not only showcase their talents but also pay tribute to the celestial event.

"We wanted to capture the essence of the eclipse and the magic it brings," West explained in an interview. "We chose to work with Stephen because he's a genius, and we knew he would bring a unique perspective to the project."

Hawking, who is known for his groundbreaking work in theoretical physics, was thrilled to be a part of the project. "I was amazed by the opportunity to combine my knowledge of the universe with Kanye's creativity," he said. "The result is a video that is both visually stunning and thought-provoking."

The video, which is set to be released on August 15th, features Jolie and Kelce performing a mesmerizing dance routine while surrounded by stunning visuals of the eclipse. West and Hawking have also incorporated a special effects-laden sequence that showcases the solar system and the universe in a way that is both educational and entertaining.

In a unique twist, West has decided to pause the release of the video for a week, starting from August 1st. During this time, he will be sharing behind-the-scenes footage and insights into the making of the video on his social media channels.

"This is a chance for my fans to get a deeper look into the creative process and appreciate the hard work that went into making this video," West said. "I'm excited to share this experience with them and hope they'll join me on this journey."
