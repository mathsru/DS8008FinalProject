Margot Robbie Had a Hilariously Embarrassing Encounter with Obama on Her Honeymoon Margot Robbie just topped your epic honeymoon story, thanks to Ellen DeGeneres, Portia de Rossi, former president Barack Obama, and her husband Tom Ackerley's super revealing pair of short shorts. Here's how it all went down.

Though she and Ackerley tied the knot in December 2016, the couple didn't embark on their newlywed getaway until after the actress filmed I, Tonya. The pair then jetted off to Tahiti for a joint vacation, which started off with a four-day stay in a secluded part of the island in a hut, sans electricity. But for the second half of their trip, they stayed at a luxury hotel.

Upon arrival, Robbie and Ackerley decided to hit the hotel gym, but for the workout, he chose to wear "his oldest gym shorts, and they're tiny," the actress recalled. "And there's nothing underneath there." It was fine though, the couple thought. Who could they possibly run into?

Lo and behold, the two find Ellen DeGeneres and her wife Portia de Rossi running on treadmills—beside Barack Obama. (The former president spent time in French Polynesia to write his book.)

But that wasn't all. The five of them also took part in a small workout class together, which was equal parts uncomfortable and hilarious, considering Ackerley's lacking attire. Ellen demonstrated just how short—and wide—his shorts were:
