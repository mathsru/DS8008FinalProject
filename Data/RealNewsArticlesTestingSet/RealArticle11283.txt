Paul Nassif Net Worth What Is Dr. Paul Nassif's Net Worth?

Specializing in rhinoplasty and facial plastic surgery, Nassif built a successful medical practice in Beverly Hills, catering to high-profile clients and individuals seeking complex reconstructive procedures. Beyond surgery, Nassif has expanded his career into entrepreneurship. He launched NassifMD Dermaceuticals, a skincare line focused on anti-aging and skin rejuvenation, which has been featured on QVC and gained a global customer base. His business ventures, combined with his television presence, have positioned him as a prominent figure in the aesthetics industry.

In addition to "Botched," Nassif has appeared on reality shows like "Dr. 90210" and "The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills," the latter through his previous marriage to Adrienne Maloof. With a combination of surgical expertise, media influence, and business acumen, Paul Nassif continues to be a leading voice in plastic surgery and cosmetic innovation.

Early Life

Paul S. Nassif, MD, was born on June 6, 1962, in Los Angeles, California. He is Lebanese-American. Nassif attended Loyola High School and the University of Southern California. He graduated in 1974. Nassif studied at Rosalind Franklin University of Medicine and Science/Chicago Medical School for the first two years of his medical education. He graduated from the University of Southern California School of Medicine in 1992, which he attended for the last two years of his general medical education.

Career

Nassif interned in general surgery residency at the University of New Mexico Health Sciences Center. He then had a residency in Otolaryngology-head and neck surgery at the University of New Mexico from 1993 to 1997. In 1998, Dr. Nassif completed a facial plastic and reconstructive surgery fellowship at St. Louis University School of Medicine in 1998.

He is licensed in both California and Nevada and practices facial plastic surgery, specifically rhinoplasty, in Beverly Hills. He sees patients from all over the world. Paul is a member of the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery.

Reality TV

Dr. Nassif also appeared in the first three seasons of "The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills" alongside his now ex-wife, Adrienne Maloof, who was a main cast member. In October 2015, it was announced that an eight-episode spin-off series of "Botched" called "Botched by Nature" would air. The show premiered on August 9, 2016. Paul has been featured on "Dr. 90210" and has appeared on "NBC Nightly News," "Inside Edition," "CBS Healthbeat," "Entertainment Tonight," "Today," "Good Morning America," and "Extra."

Other Ventures
