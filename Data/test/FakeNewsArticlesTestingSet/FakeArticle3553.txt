'The Voice' Coaches Make More Money in One Season Than We'll Probably See in Our Whole Lives How Much Do ‘The Voice’ Judges Get Paid? Coach Salaries of Adam Levine, John Legend and More

Raking it in! The Voice has had a constant rotation of coaches over the years, and they have come with a wide range of reported salaries.

Blake Shelton, for example, was the longest-running coach on The Voice until his exit after season 23. It only made sense that the country star, who was also the only coach to appear in more than 20 consecutive seasons, was also among the highest-paid celebrities on the singing competition with a reported salary of more than $13 million per season.

Still, someone has him beat: Ariana Grande, who reportedly made $20 to $25 million for her one-season stint on The Voice. This was atypical for newcomer coaches, as the reported starting salary is typically around $8 to $10 million.
