Pink Brought Her Daughter To The CMA Awards & They're The Best Couple On The Red Carpet It's not Willow's first awards show appearance this year. At the VMAs in August , Pink told a story about Willow that wowed everyone. Pink said she had to explain that there are different types of beautiful to Willow, who had told her mom she was "the ugliest girl I know." So at the CMAs, Willow got to wear the big princess dress for awhile, and then change into her normal clothes — and be a couple of different types of girl, all of which are beautiful.
