The INSIDER Summary:

19-year-old actress Ariel Winter often responds to online bullies who shame her body and outfits over social media.

Sarah Hyland told People that she and her "Modern Family" co-stars have helped Winter become body-confident.

"I tried to be a good role model for her, and a true older sister for her," she said.

Hyland also shared how she stays confident despite her own set of body-shamers.



Ariel Winter routinely shuts down online bullies who shame her for wearing "revealing" outfits and accuse her of Photoshopping her pictures to appear slimmer. However, the "Modern Family" actress wasn't always so confident.

Sarah Hyland, who plays Winter's older sister on the popular TV show, recently opened up to People about how she mentored Winter when they first met.

"I mean, she was 11 years old when I met her. I was 18," she told People. "I tried to be a good role model for her, and a true older sister for her because she’s such a sweet little girl."

Hyland is now 26, and Winter is 19.

Over the last eight years, Winter has become a target for body-shamers and slut-shamers who criticize her evolving style on social media.

However, over time, Winter has figured out how to respond perfectly to her haters. Some of that confidence is thanks to her co-stars, according to Hyland.

"I like to think we — me, Julie Bowen, and Sofia Vergara — helped her be confident with her body and herself," Hyland told People.

The actress has faced her own battle with body-shamers. In May, social media users accused Hyland of being a bad role model and glorifying eating disorders because of her weight.

Hyland responded to her critics, sharing that her personal health issues were preventing her from gaining weight.

"I've basically been on bed rest for the past few months, I've lost a lot of muscle mass," she wrote on Twitter. "My circumstances have put me in a place where I'm not in control of what my body looks like. So I strive to be as healthy as possible, as everyone should."

Despite this, Hyland tries to remain confident. The actress shared how she stays positive with People.

"It's about being confident in your self [sic], and not letting people's hateful words get to you," she said. "Just surround yourself with positivity and not let the negativity get to you."
