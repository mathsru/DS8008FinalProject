Becky G And Diego Boneta Will Host the Latin American Music Awards Trending on Billboard

Becky G will host Telemundo’s third annual Latin American Music Awards (Latin AMAs) alongside Diego Boneta on Oct. 26. The awards show will begin at 8 p.m. EST live from the Dolby Theater in Hollywood.

Becky G is 20 years old and has already earned her first No. 1 on the Billboard Latin charts with “Can’t Get Enough” featuring Pitbull, who coincidentally will become the first-ever recipient of the Latin AMA Dick Clark Achievement Award. The singer is a Mexican-American with other credits as an actress in Power Rangers, multi-platinum song “Shower” and a guest star spot in FOX Emmy-winning series Empire — her achievements earning her a spot on Billboard’s 21 Under 21 list this year. Boneta has previous appearances on American television screens through Beverly Hills 90210 and Pretty Little Liars.

Throughout the show, the following acts will perform live: Abraham Mateo, Alejandra Guzman, Angeles, Banda MS, Bad Bunny, Bacilos, Camila Cabello, Chayanne, Chiquis Rivera, Christian Nodal, Fifth Harmony, Gente de Zona, Gloria Trevi, Jesse & Joy, Manuel Medrano, Natalia Jimenez, Pablo Alboran, Pitbull, Prince Royce, and Wisin.

Fans in the United States and Puerto Rico have until Oct. 17 to vote online through www.LatinAMAs.com/vota with fans worldwide able to vote on social media through #LatinAMAs for categories varying from Favorite Crossover Artist, Favorite Collaboration, New Artist of the Year, Artist of the Year and others.
