Carrie Ann Inaba Asks Her Mom to Be Her Matron of Honor on Live TV -- See the Cute Moment! Carrie Ann Inaba's wedding plans just got sweeter!

While filling in as co-host for Kelly Ripa during LIVE With Kelly & Ryan on Tuesday, the Dancing With the Stars judge, who's engaged to actor Robb Derringer, adorably asked her mother, Patty, to be her matron of honor.

The surprise all went down when the bride-to-be started explaining to Ryan Seacrest that she was still in the process of choosing her bridal party.

"It's really hard because you have your O.G. friends, like I have my friends from Hawaii, then I have my friends from L.A., and then I have my family friends, and then I have my mom," she said.

Patty was seated in the audience for the taping and was seemingly shocked when her daughter started directing attention to her seat.

"And I wanted to ask, Mom, would you be my matron of honor?" Carrie Ann asked, as Patty became teary-eyed.

"Is that a 'yes'?" the 49-year-old choreographer joked after giving her mom a hug. "She said 'yes!' Yay, thank you, mom. I love you."

Derringer popped the question to Carrie Ann last December with a stunning Jean Dousset ring on a beach in California.
