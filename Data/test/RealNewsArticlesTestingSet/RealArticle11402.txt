Teigen looked stunning in her sparkling dress that fell to the middle of her thigh and featured a slit over her left leg.

The frock featured a plunging neckline and long sleeves and she added a pair of strappy black heels and Le Vian rings.

Her blond tresses were coiffed by professional hairstylist Glen Coco, who used tools from GHD in order to create the chic look for the evening.

'We decided to go with a 60s messy updo with flirty bangs,' Glen said of the look, which he achieved by prepping the hair with the GHD Air Hairdryer and the brand's Ceramic Radial Brush in order to create all-over volume.

From there, he curled the hair using the Curve Classic Curl Iron, before loosening the waves and adding extra texture with the Platinum+ Styler, which he explained 'makes it seem more organic and undone'.

The hair was finished off with texturizing spray, before being pinned into a loose French twist, from which Glen pulled a few strands to frame Chrissy's face.
