‘Game of Thrones’ Stars Sophie Turner, Maisie Williams Read Song Lyrics as Ned Stark (Video) 

Sophie Turner and Maisie Williams are keeping Ned Stark’s (Sean Bean) legacy alive, one “Lion King” song at a time.

In an extended preview clip from Apple Music’s next “Carpool Karaoke” episode, the two “Game of Thrones” actresses drive around town reading song lyrics from “Hakuna Matata” and Sir Mix-a-Lot’s “Baby Got Back” in the very serious voice of the former Hand of the King.

The game certainly cracked them up, at least — watch the video above. And no, it doesn’t contain any spoilers from Sunday’s Season 7 finale.

Also on Tuesday, Apple and the adapted series’ executive producer James Corden shared a multi-minute sneak peek of Seth MacFarlane and Ariana Grande’s turn in the SUV, when the duo belted out “Suddenly Seymour” from “Little Shop of Horrors.”

Oh, and they almost got in a car accident — that’s just a matter of time with this distracting gimmick.
