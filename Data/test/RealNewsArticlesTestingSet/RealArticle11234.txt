Naomi Campbell, Cindy Crawford show daughter Kaia, young models how it's done at Versace Maeve McDermott

USA TODAY

Sept. 22, 2017, 3:19 p.m. ET

Naomi, Cindy, Helena, Claudia, Carla.

While a new pack of "It" girl models have dominated runways this year, Versace's Milan show paid tribute to the supermodel O.G.s, closing with a striking tableau of Carla Bruni, Claudia Schiffer, Naomi Campbell, Cindy Crawford and Helena Christensen in golden gowns.

While it's tempting to call them the Golden Girls, there was nothing "aging" about the five iconic beauties, who stood arm in arm with Donatella Versace at the end of the show.

The presentation sought strength in numbers, with packs of three or more models walking side by side down the extra-long runway, showing off coordinated wildly-patterned looks.

Kendall Jenner and Gigi and Bella Hadid were there, of course, as was Kaia Gerber, who appeared on the runway just before her mother Cindy Crawford emerged, showing the younger generation of models how it's done.
