Kim Kardashian Returns to Paris for the First Time Since Her Robbery in 2016 The infamous 2016 jewelry heist, where she was gagged, tied up, and robbed at gunpoint of $10 million worth of jewels changed Kim Kardashian’s life entirely. It made her appreciate her loved ones and her life more than ever and caused her to—for years—simplify her style, eschewing flashy jewelry and major displays of wealth.

“After I was robbed in Paris,” she said in an episode of The Kardashians at the time, “I haven’t bought jewelry.”

But now that she’s back in the French capital to face her robbers in court, she’s no longer hiding.

Yesterday, Kardashian arrived at the Palais de Justice, accompanied by her mother, Kris Jenner, to confront the 10 people accused in the robbery. For the court appearance, nine years in the making, the Skims founder wore a dramatic black vintage gown from John Galliano, pointy slingbacks from Saint Laurent, and $7 million worth of diamond jewelry.

Kardashian forgave her robbers in court while decked out in the gleaming diamonds. “I’m obviously emotional about it. This experience changed my life, and it changed my family’s life,” she said after an apology note from one of the defendants was read aloud. “I do appreciate the letter, I forgive you for what has taken place, but it doesn’t change the emotion and the feelings and the trauma—the way my life is forever changed.”

After the court appointment, Kardashian stepped out for a lavish dinner at Le Voltaire, where she brought the drama in a luxurious brown look. This one was composed of a sheer chocolate slipdress with barely there spaghetti straps, from John Galliano, and a shaggy caramel-brown fur coat from Saint Laurent. She capped off the fit with chocolate peep-toe heels, a matching croc-embossed tote bag, and a diamond anklet.
